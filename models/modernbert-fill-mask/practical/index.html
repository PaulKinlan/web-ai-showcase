Practical: long-context masking & knowledge probing

Two things a developer would actually ship. Long-context masking fills a blank whose answer is stated far earlier in a long passage — the kind of distant dependency BERT's 512-token window simply can't see, but ModernBERT's 8,192-token context can. Knowledge probing turns a fact or a code pattern into a fill-in-the-blank and reads out what the model "knows" — and where it's unsure.
