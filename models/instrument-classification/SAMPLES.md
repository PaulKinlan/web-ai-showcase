Note: the model's accuracy is imperfect on out-of-distribution timbres (e.g. a muted trumpet or a
breathy flute), so bundled clips are provided to demonstrate real inference and the full confidence
distribution, not to imply perfect classification. The default sample (violin) classifies correctly.
