Practical: sort a sample library by instrument

A real developer job: you have a folder of loops and one-shots and you want them bucketed by instrument. Add clips (the bundled samples are pre-loaded — drop your own too), classify them all, and the app groups them into instrument buckets — entirely on-device, so copyrighted audio never leaves the machine.
