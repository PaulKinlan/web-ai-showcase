Wild: a moving scene → what changed?

Here's the thing a single-image model simply can't do. We animate a little scene on a canvas, sample a handful of frames from across it, and hand the whole sequence to SmolVLM2 at once — then ask it what happened. Temporal reasoning over frames, no server, no video upload.
