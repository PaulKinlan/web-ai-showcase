Wild: French idioms & elisions

Idioms are memorised whole — so masking the last word is a real test of whether a model has read enough French. Mask the punchline of a proverb and watch CamemBERT try to complete it. A multilingual model usually fumbles these; a French-native one has seen them thousands of times.

backend –latency –

The correct completions here are boire, dents, ours, lapin, cœur and nid. When CamemBERT nails one with high confidence, it's because it memorised the whole expression — the logit tells you how sure it is.
