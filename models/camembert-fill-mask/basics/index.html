Basics: fill the blank in French

Masked language modelling in its simplest form. Pick a French sentence — or write your own with a <mask> — and CamemBERT ranks what word belongs there, each with a probability and the raw logit behind it.

Every candidate is a French subword — CamemBERT spent its whole vocabulary on French. Click a predicted row to drop it into the sentence.
