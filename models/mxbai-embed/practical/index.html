Two jobs you'd actually ship

Once text is a vector, features become arithmetic. Here are two, running entirely on your device with mxbai: prompted semantic search (the retrieval-prompt convention, done right) and near-duplicate detection (collapse "same thing, different words"). Both are the backbone of real search and data-cleaning pipelines.

Your text (one item per line)

1 · Prompted semantic search

Rank every line by meaning against a query — the query is embedded with the retrieval prompt, the documents plain.

2 · Near-duplicate detection

Find every pair of lines whose cosine similarity is above the threshold — the "same intent, different words" pairs a keyword match would miss. Drag the threshold to tighten or loosen what counts as a duplicate.
