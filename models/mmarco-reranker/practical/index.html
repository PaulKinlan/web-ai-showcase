Practical: cross-lingual FAQ & support search

A real support desk keeps its knowledge base in whatever language each article was written in. With a multilingual cross-encoder, a customer can ask in their own language and the best answer surfaces — even if it was written in another. Pick a question language, keep the mixed-language answer bank, and the reranker scores every answer against the question directly.

backend –latency –answers –

The top answer should be the refund policy — in Spanish, Japanese or Italian depending on which one best matches — even though the question is in English. A keyword search would miss all three; the cross-encoder matches meaning across languages.
