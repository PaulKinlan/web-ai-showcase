Multi-model: classify → describe

A specialist label plus a general description. Here Food-101 names the exact dish (its whole world is food, so it's sharp), and ViT-GPT2 writes a sentence about the same plate — giving you a structured dish tag and a human-readable caption from one drop, both computed on your device.

Stage 1 — dish classifier (Food-101 Swin, ~93 MB)

Stage 2 — captioner (ViT-GPT2, ~250 MB)

Why chain them? The Food-101 model gives a precise dish label (great for search, diary logging, and nutrition lookup), while the general captioner describes the scene in a sentence (great for a person or a screen reader). One drop, two complementary outputs — a tidy on-device enrichment pipeline.
