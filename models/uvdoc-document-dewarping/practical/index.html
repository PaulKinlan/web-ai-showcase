Flatten a real page, then download the scan

A real workflow: drop your own photo of a curled or angled page, flatten it into a clean scanner-style document, and download the PNG. No upload, no cloud scanner.

Why on-device

Document scanning apps usually send your page to a server. Here it's a static web page: private, free, offline once the ~30 MB model is cached. Drop a photo of a receipt, contract or book page and the flattened scan downloads straight from the canvas — nothing was uploaded.
