Why it breaks

UVDoc estimates a single continuous deformation from one photo. Gentle curls and perspective are well within what it learned; but a sharp fold creates a discontinuity — and where the page folds back on itself, information is simply occluded and can't be recovered from one view. The correction magnitude climbs with severity, then the result starts to warp unnaturally as the model extrapolates past what it can invert.
