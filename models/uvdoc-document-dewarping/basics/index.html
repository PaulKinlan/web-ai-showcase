What you're seeing

UVDoc doesn't crop or rotate — it predicts the page's continuous 3D deformation and resamples every pixel to undo it. Watch the bowed text lines straighten and the tilted edges square up as you wipe: that's a geometric rectification a simple perspective crop could never do on a curled page.
