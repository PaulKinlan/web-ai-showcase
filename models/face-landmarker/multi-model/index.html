Face × Hands: expression mesh + finger tracking

An avatar needs a face and hands; sign language is face plus hands. Run both models on one canvas: FaceLandmarker lays down the 478-point expression mesh, and HandLandmarker layers detailed 21-point finger tracking on top. Two models, one frame, all on-device.
