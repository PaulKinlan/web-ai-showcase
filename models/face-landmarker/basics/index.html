Basics: mesh a face

The simplest thing FaceLandmarker does: find the face in a picture and lay a 478-point mesh over it. Pick a sample or drop your own photo, then read the named landmark coordinates and the head pose (yaw/pitch/roll) the model returned.

Drop a face photo, or click to choose
delegate –latency –faces –yaw –pitch –roll –
Detect a face to see its landmark coordinates.
