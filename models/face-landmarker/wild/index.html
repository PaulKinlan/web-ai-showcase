Wild: an emoji that mirrors you

Your face is the controller. This drives a big SVG emoji straight from the blendshapes — blink and it blinks, smile and it smiles, open wide and its mouth drops, raise your brows and its brows lift. No 3D rig, no calibration: just the 52 expression channels the model already hands you. Start the webcam and pull faces, or load a sample to freeze one expression.
