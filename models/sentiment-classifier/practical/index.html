Practical: triage a batch of feedback

Support inboxes and review feeds are firehoses. Paste a batch here — one message per line — and the model sorts them into positive and negative, ranked by confidence, so the angriest messages float to the top of the queue. All of it runs in the tab; no customer text is uploaded anywhere.
