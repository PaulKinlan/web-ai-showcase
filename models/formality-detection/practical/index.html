Practical: tone-check a professional draft

You're writing to a client and want it to read consistently formal. Paste the draft — it's split into sentences, each scored for register, so the one casual line that slipped in ("thanks a bunch!") stands out before you hit send. Nothing is uploaded.

backend – sentences – casual flagged – overall – latency –

Each sentence is scored independently; the casual ones are flagged so you can decide whether to reword them. The overall figure is the mean formal-probability across sentences — a quick read on whether the whole message lands where you intend, computed entirely client-side.
