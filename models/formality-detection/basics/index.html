Basics: formal or informal?

The plain capability. Type text and the model reads its register — formal or informal — and places it on a meter running from casual to formal. One forward pass, entirely in the tab.

Every keystroke re-runs the model (debounced). Register is independent of sentiment: "I regret to inform you" is negative but formal; "this is awesome!!" is positive but casual — try both.
