Practical: FAQ search & duplicate detection

The two deployments this model has powered a thousand times over. First: match a user's question to the right help answer by meaning — "my parcel never arrived" finds the shipping FAQ even though it shares no keywords. Second: catch near-duplicate tickets before a human triages them twice. Both run entirely client-side — embed once, compare with cosine.
