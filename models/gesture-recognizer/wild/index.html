Wild: rock, paper, scissors

Play the oldest game there is against your browser. Show ✊ rock, ✋ paper, or ✌️ scissors — hit Throw, and on "shoot!" the model reads your hand while the computer picks blind. Winner takes the round; it keeps score. No hardware but your webcam (or throw a sample hand to play a round without a camera).
