Gesture × Face: a reaction cam

Reactions on a video call are a gesture and a face. Run both models on one frame: GestureRecognizer names your hand gesture (👍 ✌️ ✋…) while FaceLandmarker tracks your 478-point expression mesh. Throw a thumbs-up and grin — the page reads both at once, entirely on-device.
