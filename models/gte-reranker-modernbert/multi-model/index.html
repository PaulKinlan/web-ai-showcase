Multi-model: GTE-ModernBERT retrieves, the reranker reorders

The two-stage pipeline behind real search and RAG, running entirely in your browser with two models that share one backbone. Stage 1: the GTE-ModernBERT bi-encoder embeds every passage and the query and ranks by cosine similarity — fast, but only surface-level. Stage 2: the GTE-reranker-ModernBERT cross-encoder reads each shortlisted (query, passage) pair together and reorders for precision. Same ModernBERT architecture, two different heads — a bi-encoder for recall, a cross-encoder for precision. Compare the two orders below.
