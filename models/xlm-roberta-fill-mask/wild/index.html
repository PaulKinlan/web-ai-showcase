Wild: analogies, across languages

The classic word-analogy trick — "man is to woman as king is to queen" — expressed as a masked sentence. Because XLM-RoBERTa shares one space across languages, you can even cross them: "London is to English as Berlin is to <mask>". Pick a pattern or write your own.

backend –latency –

Analogies are not the model's training objective, so expect surprises — it is guessing the word that best fits the pattern in context, not doing vector arithmetic. That's part of the fun: the logits show how sure it is.
