Denoise, then transcribe

Speech enhancement isn't just for listening — it helps machines too. Here GTCRN denoises the noisy clip, then Whisper transcribes both the noisy and the enhanced audio. Against the known reference, the word-error-rate should drop after enhancement. Two real models, chained on-device.
