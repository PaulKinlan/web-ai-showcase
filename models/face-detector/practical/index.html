Where this goes

This is the pattern behind "blur bystanders" in a screenshot tool, redacting faces in user-generated uploads before they're stored, or anonymising CCTV/dashcam clips for sharing. Because detection and blurring both happen client-side, the un-redacted image never has to be transmitted — a real privacy guarantee, not a promise. Note BlazeFace short-range is tuned for near faces; distant faces in a wide shot may be missed, so treat it as a strong assist, not a certified redactor.
