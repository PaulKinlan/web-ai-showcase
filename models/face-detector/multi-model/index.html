Detect × Mesh: the two-stage face pipeline

This is the classic pipeline, both halves running live: a fast FaceDetector boxes where the faces are, then FaceLandmarker lays its 478-point mesh over the primary face for detailed analysis. Detect first, analyse second — two MediaPipe models, one canvas, all on-device.

The detector boxes every face it finds; the landmarker (set to one face here) meshes the primary one. On a crowd photo you'll see many boxes and one mesh.
