Multi-model: translate, then speak Urdu

Two models, one pipeline, zero servers. M2M100 (multilingual, English→Urdu) translates what you type into native right-to-left Urdu script, then MMS-TTS Urdu reads the translation aloud. Type English, get spoken Urdu — entirely on your device.
