Basics: score two sentences

The simplest thing the model does: take two sentences and return one graded number — 0 (unrelated) to 5 (essentially identical). Try the samples to walk down the scale.

One number, but a graded one — that's the whole point of a similarity regressor. The next rung turns that number into a real decision with a threshold.
