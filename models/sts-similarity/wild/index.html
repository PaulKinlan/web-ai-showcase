Wild: negation traps and near-paraphrases

The interesting cases are where surface overlap lies. Some pairs share almost every word but mean opposite things (negation, swapped subject/object); others share almost no words but mean the same. A keyword or bag-of-words score gets these backwards — watch the cross-encoder read the meaning. Each sample shows the word-overlap next to the model's score so you can see the gap.
