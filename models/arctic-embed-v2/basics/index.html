Basics: one number for "same meaning?"

The simplest thing arctic-embed does: turn two passages into 768-number vectors and measure the cosine between them. Same meaning across languages, or a faithful paraphrase, scores high; unrelated text scores low — even when the two are written in different languages. Edit either box and re-run.

cosine – verdict – backend – latency –

Cosine runs from −1 to 1; for arctic-embed dense vectors, cross-lingual paraphrases typically land around 0.7–0.85 and unrelated pairs well below 0.4. There's no hard threshold — it's a smooth similarity, and you pick the cut-off that fits your task.
