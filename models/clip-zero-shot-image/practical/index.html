Practical: auto-tagging & moderation

The shippable version. You define a fixed label set — your tag vocabulary or your moderation policy — and CLIP scores every image against it. No dataset, no fine-tuning, no server: the whole classifier is a list of words plus a threshold you own.

Auto-tag mode uses the raw cosine similarity per tag and a threshold, so an image can get many tags (or none) — the honest way to do multi-label. Moderation mode uses the softmax across mutually-exclusive policy classes and takes the top one as the verdict.
