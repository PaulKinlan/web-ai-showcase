Wild: personas & visible reasoning

Phi-4-mini is small but a genuinely capable reasoner. Give it a persona, hand it a puzzle, and ask it to think out loud — the model works through the problem step by step before committing to an answer, and you watch the chain of thought stream in real time. All on your GPU, nothing uploaded.

Why "think step by step" works

Asking a model to show its working — chain-of-thought prompting — gives it room to break a hard problem into smaller ones it can each get right, rather than blurting a single guess. It's the same model either way; the system prompt just changes how it uses its tokens. Bigger reasoners like Phi-4-mini benefit most, which is why the answers here hold up on puzzles a 0.5B model would fumble.
