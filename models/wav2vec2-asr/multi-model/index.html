Multi-model: CTC transcript → LLM cleanup

wav2vec2's vocabulary is only letters, an apostrophe and word boundaries — so its output is ALL-CAPS with no punctuation. That's a perfect job to hand to a second model: a small language model (Qwen2.5-0.5B) restores sentence casing and punctuation without changing the words. Two models chained in your browser.
