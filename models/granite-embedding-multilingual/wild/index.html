Wild: cluster mixed-language passages into themes

No labels, no training, no translation. Embed a jumble of passages — here in English, French, Spanish and German — into the shared 384-d space, then run k-means over the vectors. Passages about the same thing pull together regardless of language, and a 2D projection lets you see the groups. This is unsupervised, cross-lingual topic discovery, entirely in the browser.

k-means needs you to pick k up front, and the 2D map is a lossy squash of 384 dimensions — so treat both as an exploration tool, not ground truth. Move the k slider and re-cluster to feel how the grouping shifts; a good k usually makes the map's blobs line up with the coloured clusters.
