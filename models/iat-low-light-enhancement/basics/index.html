The plain capability: take a normal photo, under-expose it by a known amount, and let IAT recover the light. Drag the divider to wipe between dark and enhanced.

What you're seeing

IAT doesn't just multiply brightness — it predicts a per-pixel illumination correction, so the dark corners get lifted more than the parts that were already lit. Watch the brightness number climb and detail reappear in the shadows that a flat “+brightness” slider would either miss or blow out.
