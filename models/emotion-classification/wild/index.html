Wild: the emotional arc of a poem or song

Writing moves. Paste a poem, a verse, or song lyrics and this scores each line for its dominant emotion, then draws the arc — a strip of coloured stops you can read like a heartbeat. See a lyric climb from sadness into joy, or a poem turn on a single line. All on-device, one batched pass.

lines –backend –total time –

The model reads each line in isolation, so it catches local turns a whole-document score would average away. That's what makes an arc: the emotion of line 4 doesn't have to agree with line 1. Try your own lyrics — the swing from the last two lines is usually the fun part.
