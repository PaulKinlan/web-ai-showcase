Multi-model: build a structured record, then tag its topic

Turn a free-text paragraph in any language into a structured record. XLM-RoBERTa NER extracts the people, organizations, locations and dates into typed lists; mDeBERTa zero-shot then tags the passage with a topic you define. Two multilingual models, one pipeline, all on-device.
