Wild: the round-trip — draw it, then describe it

Because one model both draws and reads, it can close the loop on itself: give it a prompt, it generates an image, and then the same model looks at what it just made and tells you what it sees. Compare your prompt to its own read-back — a little window into what the model actually rendered.
