Multi-model: clinical fill, MiniLM ranks by meaning

Fill-mask ranks candidates by how likely they are in context — not by what they mean. So compose two models: Bio_ClinicalBERT proposes candidate terms for a clinical blank, then all-MiniLM-L6 embeds each completed sentence and re-ranks them by cosine similarity to a target meaning you choose. It surfaces the fills that actually match clinical intent — a tiny, private, on-device pipeline.

Two models, both on-device

Bio_ClinicalBERT (~131 MB) proposes; MiniLM embeddings (~25 MB) ranks. Each auto-initialises from cache when present.
