Basics: fill a clinical mask

Masked language modelling in its simplest form. Put a [MASK] in a clinical or biomedical sentence and Bio_ClinicalBERT ranks the words that fit — each with a probability and the exact logit it computed. Because it was trained on clinical notes, the ranking reads like a clinician's expectation, not the open web's.
