Why on-device

Denoising a photo usually means a cloud API or a desktop editor. Here it's a static web page: private, free, offline once the ~75 MB model is cached. Drop your own low-light shot and the cleaned PNG downloads straight from the canvas — nothing was uploaded to produce it.
