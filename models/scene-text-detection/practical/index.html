The redacted image is produced entirely in-page from the detected boxes — nothing is uploaded. This blacks out every detected region; a real tool would let you keep the parts you want.

Why detect-then-redact beats OCR-then-redact

You don't need to read a receipt to hide it — you only need to know where its text is. Skipping recognition means the sensitive characters are never decoded at all: the model outputs regions, the page fills them with black, done. Cheaper, faster, and it leaks nothing.
