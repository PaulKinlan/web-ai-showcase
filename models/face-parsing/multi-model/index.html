Multi-model: depth-shaded recolour

Compose two on-device models. Face Parsing says which pixels are hair or lips; Depth Anything v2 says how near each pixel is. Multiply them and a recolour becomes a pseudo-3D makeover — the tint is strongest where the part bulges toward the camera and fades where it recedes, so flat colour reads as shaped. Both models run locally; two downloads, then everything composites instantly.

Model 1 — Face Parsing (~90 MB)

Model 2 — Depth Anything v2 small (~100 MB, WebGPU/WASM)
