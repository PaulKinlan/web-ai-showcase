CLAP × Whisper: triage, then transcribe

A real pipeline of two local models. CLAP first decides what a clip is, scoring it against labels you control. If it lands on speech, it hands the audio to Whisper, which writes down the words. Anything else — music, a siren, a tone — just gets tagged. That's how you'd triage a stream of audio without wasting an ASR model on non-speech, and without a server.
