Mad-Libs, powered by grammar

Mad-Libs works because you can blank a word by its role without reading the sentence. That's exactly what a POS tagger gives you. Pick a part of speech, and every word of that role is knocked out and turned into a fill-in box. Type whatever you like — the sillier the better — and rebuild a brand-new sentence. No word lists, no hand-marking: the model finds the slots.
