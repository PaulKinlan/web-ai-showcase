Arithmetic on meaning: A − B + C ≈ ?

The famous word2vec trick — king − man + woman ≈ queen — was vector arithmetic on embeddings. GTE is a sentence embedder, not word2vec, so it is not built for this. But the geometry still bends toward the answer. Pick three terms, and we compute vec(A) − vec(B) + vec(C), then rank a vocabulary by cosine to that target. You see the real nearest neighbours — hit or miss.
