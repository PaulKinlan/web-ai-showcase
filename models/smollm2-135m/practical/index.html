Practical: a one-shot rewriter

Rewriting a short message to a chosen tone is exactly the kind of small, bounded task a 135M model handles well. Paste some text, pick a tone, and get a cleaned-up version — generated entirely on your device, no server, no key.

Why this is a good fit for a tiny model

Rewriting is a transformation, not a knowledge task: the answer is already in the input, and the model just re-expresses it. That plays to a small model's strengths and away from its weakness (it doesn't need to know much). Because it runs on-device, the text you're rewriting — which may be private — never leaves the tab.
