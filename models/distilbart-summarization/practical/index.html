Practical: TL;DR a document or meeting

Drop in a long email thread, a doc, or a meeting transcript and get the gist as a paragraph or as bullet points. Because it runs on-device, you can safely summarize private or internal material — nothing is uploaded.

Format
TL;DR
in→out –compression – backend –time –

Bullet mode splits the generated summary into sentences — honest post-processing, not a different model. For very long inputs, summarize section by section and then summarize the summaries (see the Wild demo for the recursive version).
