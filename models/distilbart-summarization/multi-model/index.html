Recap a recording: speech → transcript → summary

Two browser models in one pipeline. Whisper turns audio into a transcript; then DistilBART boils that transcript down to a short recap. Pick a clip or upload your own — audio in, TL;DR out, and nothing ever leaves the device.

Each model initialises on its own below (cached copies auto-start, no button).

Why this is the interesting part

Transcription and summarization are each useful alone. Chained, they're a template for a whole class of tool: a private meeting recorder that hands you the action items, a lecture note-taker, a voicemail triager, a podcast digest — all running with no server, no API bill, and no audio leaving the tab. Drop the sentiment or translation models into the chain and you can route, localise, or tag the recap too.
