Basics: point at the answer

The simplest thing extractive QA does: read a short passage, take a question, and highlight the exact words that answer it. Pick a question or type your own and watch the span light up in the passage.

Because the answer is always a span of the passage, the model can't invent a fact that isn't there — it can only point at what's in front of it. That's the core guarantee of extractive QA.
