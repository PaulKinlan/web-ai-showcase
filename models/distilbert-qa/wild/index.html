Wild: catching the questions it can't answer

A SQuAD-1.1 model has a blind spot: it was only ever trained on answerable questions, so it always returns its best span — even when the passage doesn't contain the answer at all. That's dangerous for a real bot. The fix is to watch its confidence: when the start-token distribution is diffuse (no token clearly begins an answer), it's probably guessing. Try an unanswerable question and watch the confidence gauge collapse.
