Practical: rough notes → a tidy draft

A concrete on-device task: paste messy notes and have LFM2 reshape them. Pick a target format — a polite email, a one-paragraph summary, or a bullet list — and the model rewrites, streaming the result. All local: sensitive text never leaves the tab.
