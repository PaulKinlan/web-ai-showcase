The polyglot persona engine

The system prompt is a costume — and Falcon3 speaks several languages, so the costume can include a language. Pick a persona, or write your own, and the very same on-device 1B model becomes a pirate, a hard-boiled detective, or a Parisian café owner who replies in French. It's a vivid way to feel how much of an LLM's "personality" is just conditioning, all happening in your tab.

What to notice

Nothing about the weights changes between personas — only the first message in the context. Higher temperature (this page uses 0.85) gives the voice more room to play, and because Falcon3 is multilingual you can ask a persona to answer in another language and it will. It's the same mechanism a product uses to give an assistant a brand voice, done fully on-device.
