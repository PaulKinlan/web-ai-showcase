Multi-model: matte, then classify the subject

BEN2 cuts the subject out; MobileNet v2 then classifies just the subject on a clean backdrop — so the label describes the object, not a busy background. We classify the original photo and the cutout, side by side, to show how removing the background can sharpen a classifier's answer. Two models, composed live.

This page composes two on-device models (~233 MB combined, cached after first load). Each initialises on its own below — a cached copy auto-starts, no button.
