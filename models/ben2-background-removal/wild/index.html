Wild: hair & fur edge stress test

Matting lives or dies at the edge. Hair, fur and wisps are where a soft alpha matte beats a hard on/off mask. Run BEN2, then drag the hardness slider to threshold the matte — watch the fine detail disappear as you force it toward a binary mask. The soft-edge count is real, straight off the model's output.

No WebGPU adapter here — BEN2 runs on WASM, so a single matte can take ~30–60 s. Slider tweaks afterwards are instant.

As you raise hardness, mid-range alpha values snap to 0 or 255 and the soft-edge pixel count collapses — that vanishing detail is exactly what BEN2's confidence-guided matting preserves and a hard segmentation mask throws away.
