Basics: label a scene

Semantic segmentation in its simplest form. Give SegFormer-B0 a photo and it assigns every pixel to one of 150 scene classes, painting a colour-coded map over your image. Slide the opacity to fade between the photo and the pure map, and read the legend to see what it found.
