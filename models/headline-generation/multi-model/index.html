Summarise, then headline

Long articles bury the point, and a title model does better on a tight input. So chain two on-device models: DistilBART first condenses the article to a few sentences, then the T5 title model writes a headline from that summary. Two downloads, one pipeline, zero servers — the whole thing runs in your browser. Compare the summary-fed headline against one written straight from the full article.
