What you're seeing

The model turns each clip into a 512-D embedding; the page reports the cosine of the two. The same speaker's clips point in nearly the same direction (high cosine) even when the words differ; different speakers point apart. The number is a signal, not a verdict — see the Wild demo for where it's fooled.
