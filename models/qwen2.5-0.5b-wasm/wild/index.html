The persona engine

The system prompt is a costume. Pick one — or write your own — and the very same CPU-only 0.5B model becomes a pirate, a hard-boiled detective, a Shakespearean bard. It's a vivid way to feel how much of an LLM's "personality" is just conditioning, all happening in your tab.

What to notice

Nothing about the weights changes between personas — only the first message in the context. Higher temperature (this page uses 0.85) gives the voice more room to play. It's the same mechanism a product uses to give an assistant a brand voice, done fully on-device.
