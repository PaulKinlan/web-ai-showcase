Multi-model: panoptic region → CLIP

Panoptic tells you where each object is; CLIP tells you what it is against any vocabulary you invent. Chain them: parse the scene, pick a segment, and CLIP zero-shot classification scores that one region against your labels — "a tabby cat / a black cat", "wooden / metal", a mood, a brand. Two models, one tab, nothing uploaded.

Two models load here — DETR panoptic (~45 MB) and CLIP (~90 MB). Each auto-initialises if already cached.
