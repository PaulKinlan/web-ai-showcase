Basics: parse a scene into pieces

Panoptic segmentation in its simplest form. Give the model an image and it splits every pixel into a labelled segment — each countable object on its own, plus the background regions. Blend the colour map over the photo with the opacity slider.

Drop an image, or click to choose
0.55
backend –latency –segments –

The model runs once; the slider just re-blends the segments it already found. A "thing" is a countable object (two cats = two segments); "stuff" is an amorphous region (a wall, the floor).
