Basics: the emotion of a message

The simplest thing the model does: take a sentence and tell you what it feels like — across all 28 GoEmotions classes at once. Type a message, and the ranked list shows every emotion the model detects, strongest first. Try one with mixed feelings and watch two or three light up together.

Notice there's no single "winner" being forced. Because the head is multi-label, a heartfelt thank-you can be gratitude and relief at once — the model reports both, each with its own confidence.
