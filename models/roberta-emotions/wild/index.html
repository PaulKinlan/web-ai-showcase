Wild: the emotional arc of a story

Stories move. Paste one and the model scores each sentence in turn, then plots the arc — a curve of positive and negative feeling from the first line to the last, with the dominant emotion labelled at every step. Watch dread build, break, and resolve — as a shape.

Because scoring is multi-label, each sentence carries its whole emotional chord, not a single note — the arc traces the strongest one, but hover the steps below to see the runners-up. Plain reportage stays flat; a good story swings.
