Practical: emotion-based ticket routing

A star rating tells you whether a customer is happy; the emotion tells you what to do. Paste a queue of messages (one per line) and each is scored and routed by its dominant emotion — anger/disgust → escalate, gratitude/admiration → praise, confusion/curiosity → needs help, everything else → general. One batched pass, on-device.

Routing on emotion catches things a rating can't: a calm 3-star that's actually confused (send docs) versus a 3-star that's angry (escalate now). The whole queue is scored in a single forward pass, so it scales to hundreds of messages without a single server call.
