How broken can it be?

Because this model regenerates the sentence rather than flagging spans, it can attempt text a spellchecker would give up on: heavy ESL, no punctuation, dropped words, keyboard-mash. Sometimes it nails it; sometimes it drops a clause or guesses. You see the real output either way — that honest limit is part of the point. Watch it stream, with the tokens and decode timeline.
