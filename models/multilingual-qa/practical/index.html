Practical: a multilingual FAQ box

Point it at a help-centre article — in whatever language you publish it — and let users ask questions in that language. The model pulls the exact answering sentence out of your text. One model covers every locale you ship, and the whole thing runs on-device, so it works on your docs without a search back-end or a per-language model.
