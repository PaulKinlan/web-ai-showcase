Why on-device

Turning photos into line drawings is normally a cloud API or a Photoshop action. Here it's a static web page: private, free, offline once the ~17 MB model is cached. Higher "detail" runs the model at a larger size for crisper lines (and takes a little longer on WASM). The downloaded PNG is drawn straight from the canvas — nothing left your device.
