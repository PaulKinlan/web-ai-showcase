What you're seeing

Informative Drawings doesn't threshold edges — it was trained to draw the lines a person would draw: outlines and structure that convey the scene's geometry, while ignoring noisy texture. That's why the result reads like a sketch rather than a Canny edge map. Watch how the lines land on real object boundaries as you wipe between the photo and the drawing.
