Line-art + depth: structure two ways

Two ways to read a scene's structure from one photo: Informative Drawings traces its contours, and Depth Anything estimates its distance. Run both and compare — the lines show what the shapes are, the depth shows where they sit. Both models run entirely on your device.

Both models run on-device; the photo, the drawing and the depth map never leave the tab.

Why chain them

Line-art and depth are two complementary "structure" views of a single image. A creative tool might use the line drawing as a ControlNet conditioning image and the depth map for relighting or parallax; a vision pipeline might combine crisp contours with metric layout. Extracting both locally means the original photo never has to leave the device.
