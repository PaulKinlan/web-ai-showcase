Basics: box every object, no NMS

Detection in its simplest form. Give YOLOv10 an image, and its one-to-one head draws a labelled box around every object it recognises, each with a confidence score — in a single pass, with no Non-Max-Suppression clean-up. Slide the threshold to decide how sure it has to be.

The model runs once; the slider just filters the boxes it already found. Because the head is NMS-free, even at a low threshold you get distinct objects, not a pile of overlapping duplicates.
