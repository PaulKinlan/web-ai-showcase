Multi-model: detect, crop, then classify finely

YOLOv10 is great at where and roughly what — 80 COCO classes. Pair it with a ViT image classifier and you get fine what: YOLOv10 boxes a "dog", you click the box, and a second model names the breed from 1,000 ImageNet classes. Two models, chained locally.

1 · Detector (YOLOv10, ~9 MB)

2 · Classifier (ViT, ~88 MB)

Drop an image, or click to choose

Pick a detected object to classify its crop:

detect –classify –backend –
