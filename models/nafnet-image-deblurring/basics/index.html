What you're seeing

NAFNet doesn't just boost contrast — it learned what un-blurred scenes look like and reconstructs the edges the blur destroyed. Watch the sharpness number climb, and notice detail (window frames, railings, lettering) reappear that a simple "sharpen" slider could never recover.
