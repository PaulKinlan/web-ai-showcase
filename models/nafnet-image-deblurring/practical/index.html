Rescue a shaky document photo

You photographed a receipt, a whiteboard, a page — and it came out blurry. NAFNet can make the text legible again, entirely on your device, and you can download the fixed image.

Run it

Try the text-heavy sample (blurred to mimic a shaky capture), or drop your own blurry document photo. Restore, compare, then save.

The photo and the restored image never leave your device — the download is a local file, not an upload.

Deblur, then read

Restoration is a great front door to OCR: a sharper image gives a text recogniser cleaner strokes to work with. See the multi-model demo to chain NAFNet into the on-device text detector — deblur first, then find the text the blur was hiding.
