Practical: fast voice commands

A hands-free control layer. Speak a short phrase; Moonshine transcribes it and the page matches it to the closest command and "runs" it. Because Moonshine is low-latency, a short command comes back quickly — the timing readout is real.

Try saying one of these (or something close):

  • lights "turn on the lights" / "lights off"
  • play "play some music"
  • pause "pause" / "stop"
  • weather "what's the weather"
  • timer "set a five minute timer"
No microphone here.

Record needs mic access (unavailable in headless/embedded views). Upload a short spoken phrase instead, or open in a normal tab and allow the mic.
