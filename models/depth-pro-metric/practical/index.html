Practical: measure distance between two points

Because DepthPro's depth field is on a consistent metric scale, you can compare distances across a scene — not just guess "nearer / further". Estimate depth once, then tap two points on the photo: the page reads DepthPro's depth at each and tells you which is nearer and by how much. This is the core of a tape-measure-from-a-photo tool. Needs a WebGPU device.
