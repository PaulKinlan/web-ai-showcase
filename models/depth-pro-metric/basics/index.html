Basics: a photo in, a sharp depth map out

The whole idea in its plainest form. One photo goes in; a distance-per-pixel map comes out. Warmer / brighter is nearer, darker is further. DepthPro keeps object boundaries crisp and its values on a consistent metric scale — that sharp, absolute depth is the raw signal everything else on this model is built from. Needs a WebGPU device.

There is no "right" colour — the map is only a way to read the numbers. Grayscale is the honest raw output; turbo and viridis just make the near/far bands easier to see. Notice how crisp the edges between near and far stay.
