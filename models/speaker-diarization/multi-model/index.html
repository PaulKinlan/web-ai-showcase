Multi-model: speaker-attributed transcript

Diarization tells you who and when; a recogniser tells you what. Put them together and you get a labelled transcript. wav2vec2 transcribes the audio with per-word timings, pyannote produces speaker segments, and we attribute each word to whoever was speaking at that moment — an end-to-end "Speaker 1: … / Speaker 2: …" pipeline, all on-device.
