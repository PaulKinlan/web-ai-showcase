Practical: real-time webcam tagging

This is what a mobile backbone is for. Point your camera at things around you and MobileNet labels them live, frame after frame, on-device. It's small and fast enough to keep up with a video feed — a transformer would choke. No camera? It loops a sample image instead.

Each frame is downscaled to 224×224, sent to the worker, and classified — the loop is paced so it never runs two inferences at once, and it pauses when the tab is hidden.
