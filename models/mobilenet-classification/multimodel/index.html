Multi-model: fast prefilter → ViT confirm

The classic production pattern. MobileNet triages every image in a few milliseconds; if it's already confident, you're done. Only when it's unsure do you pay for the heavyweight — escalating to ViT to confirm. You get most of the big model's accuracy at a fraction of the average cost. Loading ViT downloads ~88 MB the first time.
