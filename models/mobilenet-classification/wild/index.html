Wild: the degradation torture test

How little of an image does MobileNet actually need? Downscale a picture to a handful of pixels and crush it with JPEG artefacts, then re-classify. Push the sliders until the prediction finally breaks — you'll be surprised how far a tiny model coasts on blurry, low-res input, which is exactly why it works on cheap camera frames.
