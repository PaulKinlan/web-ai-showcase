Multi-model: ALBERT proposes, MiniLM ranks by meaning

Fill-mask ranks candidates by how likely they are in context — not by what they mean. So compose two small models: ALBERT (~45 MB) generates the candidate words for the blank, then all-MiniLM-L6 (~25 MB) embeds each completed sentence and re-ranks them by cosine similarity to a target sentence you choose. A grammatically likely fill can be semantically miles from what you meant — and both models run locally, ~70 MB total.
