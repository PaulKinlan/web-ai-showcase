Basics: fill the blank

The one thing a masked language model does: you hide a word behind [MASK], and it ranks the whole vocabulary by what belongs there. Each row shows the word, the probability, and the raw logit ALBERT actually computed — all on your device, no server.

backend – latency –

The probabilities come from softmax over 30,000 logits at the masked position. A confident slot (a proper noun, a fixed idiom) puts most of the mass on one token; an open slot spreads it thin. ALBERT is a tiny model, so it's often less peaked than BERT — informative in itself.
