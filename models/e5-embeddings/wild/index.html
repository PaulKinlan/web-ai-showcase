Wild: cluster a pile of text into themes

Give E5 a jumble of sentences with no labels and it will still find the structure. Embed everything, run k-means over the vectors, and the themes fall out on their own — support issues here, refunds there, praise in its own corner. Then see the whole set arranged on a 2D map where distance means dissimilarity. All on-device, no training, no labels.

One item per line. Everything is embedded with the passage: prefix.
