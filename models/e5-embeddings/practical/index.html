Two jobs you'd actually ship

Once text is a vector, product features become arithmetic. Here are two, running entirely on your device with E5 and its prefixes applied the right way: asymmetric semantic search (the query and the documents get different prefixes) and classification by nearest label, which needs no training data at all.

Your text (one item per line)

1 · Asymmetric semantic search

The query is embedded with query:, every line with passage: — E5's intended retrieval setup. Rank by meaning, no keyword overlap required.

2 · Classify by nearest label (zero training)

Embed a handful of label names once (as query:), then tag every line by whichever label vector it sits closest to. Change the labels and everything re-tags instantly — no retraining.
