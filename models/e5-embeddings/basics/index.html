Basics: do two lines mean the same thing?

The atom of everything embeddings do: turn two sentences into vectors and measure the angle between them. Cosine 1.00 = same direction (same meaning); near 0 = unrelated. E5 asks you to say whether each side is a query: or a passage: — flip the prefix below and watch the number move.
