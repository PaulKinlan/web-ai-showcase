Basics: classify text in its own language

The atom of multilingual zero-shot. Pick a language sample — the text and the labels are both in that language — and the model scores how well each label fits, with no training and no fixed categories. Change the labels to anything you like. The top bar is the winning category; open "see inside" for the entailment reasoning.
