Wild: one English label set, text in any language

The part that feels like a trick. Because mDeBERTa learned natural-language inference across languages, your labels don't have to match the text's language. Keep one English label set and throw Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Russian, or Portuguese at it — the model still lands each message on the right topic. Classify the whole multilingual set below, or paste your own foreign-language text. Everything runs on-device.

Cross-lingual transfer is strong but not magic: it works best with a small set of clearly distinct labels. Very fine-grained or overlapping labels, or short/ambiguous text, can still trip it up — that's why the "see inside" entailment view on the model page is worth a look.
