Multi-model: detect the language, then classify

A real multilingual pipeline is two steps: know what language you're looking at, then decide what it's about. Here an XLM-RoBERTa language detector identifies the text's language, and mDeBERTa then classifies it against your labels. The detected language is shown alongside the routing decision — the kind of two-model chain you'd wire into a support desk. Both models run on-device.
