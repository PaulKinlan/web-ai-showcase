Wild: strange labels & multi-label overlap

Zero-shot doesn't care whether your labels are sensible categories — it just measures entailment. So you can ask genuinely weird questions: is this text "written by someone in a hurry"? Does it "sound like a threat"? Flip on multi-label and each label is scored independently, so overlapping tags all light up at once instead of competing. Try the presets, then invent your own bizarre buckets.

backend –latency –mode –

With multi-label on, the bars are independent probabilities — several can be high at once, which is the whole point: one message can be hurried and urgent and funny. Switch it off and the same labels are forced to compete via softmax. Watch how the ranking changes.
