Practical: live occupancy counter

Count how many of one thing are in view, live. Pick a class — person, car, cup, whatever you're watching — and RT-DETR tallies it every frame, tracking the current count, the running peak, and a little history sparkline. This is the shape of a real footfall, parking, or queue monitor: fast enough for video, and nothing ever leaves the device.
