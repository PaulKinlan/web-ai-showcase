Wild: full-frame live webcam detector

This is RT-DETR doing the thing it was built for. Point your camera at your desk, your room, the street — and it boxes and counts everything it recognises, frame after frame, at the fastest loop your device sustains. Classic DETR is too heavy for this; RT-DETR runs it in a tab. Every frame is grabbed, detected, and drawn locally; nothing is uploaded. No camera? Scan a sample instead.
