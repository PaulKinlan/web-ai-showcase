What you're seeing

MicroDehazeNet doesn't just crank contrast — it estimates how much atmosphere sits in front of each pixel and subtracts it, following the physics of how haze forms. Watch the contrast number climb back toward the clear scene's own value, and notice distant structure and colour reappear that a flat "auto-contrast" could never recover cleanly.
