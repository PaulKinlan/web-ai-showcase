Why on-device

Batch-clearing foggy imagery normally means a server round-trip per image. Here the whole thing runs in the tab: private, free, and offline once the ~4 MB model is cached. Drop your own shot and the cleared PNG downloads straight from the canvas — nothing was uploaded to produce it.
