Multi-model: name it, box it, then classify it finely

YOLO-World is great at where and which of your words — you supply the classes. Pair it with a ViT image classifier for fine what: type "cat", YOLO-World boxes the cat, you click the box, and a second model names the breed from 1,000 ImageNet classes. Open-vocab localisation feeding fine-grained recognition — two models, chained locally.

1 · Open-vocab detector (YOLO-World, ~176 MB)

2 · Fine classifier (ViT, ~88 MB)

Drop an image, or click to choose

Pick a detected object to classify its crop:

detect –classify –backend –
