Wild: STS gauge, paraphrase mining & odd-one-out

Three things that only feel possible once a retrieval embedder runs in a tab. Rate how similar two sentences are on the familiar 0–5 STS scale. Mine paraphrases — drop in a pile of lines and pull out every matching pair. And spot the odd one out — the line that doesn't belong with the rest.

1 · STS gauge — how similar, 0 to 5?

Semantic Textual Similarity benchmarks rate pairs 0 (unrelated) to 5 (identical meaning). We map BGE's cosine onto that scale so the number feels human.

– / 5

2 · Paraphrase mining

Every pair whose cosine clears the threshold, strongest first — how you'd find duplicate FAQ entries, restated requirements, or repeated feedback across a pile.

3 · Odd one out

Which line least belongs? Each item's average similarity to the others is computed; the lowest is the outlier.
