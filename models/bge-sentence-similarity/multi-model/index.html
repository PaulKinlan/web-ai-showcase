Multi-model: retrieve with BGE, rerank with a cross-encoder

The workhorse pattern behind good search — and both halves run locally. Stage 1: BGE embeds the whole corpus and the query and pulls a shortlist by cosine similarity — fast, and it scales to thousands of docs. Stage 2: the MS-MARCO cross-encoder reads each shortlisted passage together with the query and reorders them precisely. Watch the ranking change.
