Practical: on-device inventory counter

Point a small, fast detector at a stack of photos and let it count for you. YOLOS-Tiny scans each image, tallies everything it recognises by class, and totals the one you care about — shelf facings, cars in a lot, people in a room. It's small enough to bundle into a PWA, and every photo stays on the device.
