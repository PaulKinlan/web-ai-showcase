Practical: read a chart or document, answer a question

Phi-3.5-vision's strong suit is reading and reasoning. Drop a chart, receipt, invoice, form, or app screenshot and ask a question — "which quarter was highest?", "what's the total, and does it add up?", "which field is empty?" — and it transcribes and reasons over what it sees, on your device, with nothing uploaded.
