Wild: compare two images at once

Most small VLMs take one image. Phi-3.5-vision takes several in a single turn — so you can give it two charts, two receipts, or before/after screenshots and ask what changed. Both images are tokenised into <|image_1|> and <|image_2|> and read together, entirely on your GPU.
