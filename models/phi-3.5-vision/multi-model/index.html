Multi-model: read then summarise

Two models, one job. Phi-3.5-vision (vision-language, WebGPU) reads out the content of a chart or document image, then DistilBART (a small summariser, on CPU/WASM) condenses that extract into a one-line digest — an end-to-end, on-device "image → gist" pipeline with no server.
