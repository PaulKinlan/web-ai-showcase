Basics: fill the blank character

Masked language modelling in its simplest form. Pick a Chinese sentence — or write your own with a [MASK] — and the whole-word-masked model ranks which character belongs there, each with a probability and the raw logit behind it.

Each candidate is a single Chinese character — the model reasons one Hànzì at a time. Click a predicted row to drop it into the sentence.
