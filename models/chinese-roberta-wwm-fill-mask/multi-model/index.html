Multi-model: two Chinese BERTs vote

Both Chinese BERTs share the identical 21,128-character vocabulary, so their predictions for the same [MASK] live in the same space and can be combined. Here we run the mask through whole-word-masked chinese-roberta-wwm-ext AND vanilla bert-base-chinese, then average their probabilities — an on-device ensemble. Where they agree, confidence stacks; where the training recipe pulls them apart, you see it. Two models, chained locally, nothing leaving the tab.
