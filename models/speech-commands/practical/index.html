Practical: hands-free voice control

Keyword spotting is the cheapest useful voice UI: a handful of words, recognised instantly, with no server. Start listening and say up, down, left, right to move the dot, or stop to centre it. No mic? The sample buttons drive it too.

In live mode the mic is tapped into a rolling ~1-second window and classified every fraction of a second in a Web Worker — the audio never leaves your device. Movement commands above the confidence threshold nudge the dot; everything else is ignored.
