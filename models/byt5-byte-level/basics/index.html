Byte in, byte out

The whole idea of ByT5 in one screen: whatever you type is UTF-8 encoded to bytes, each byte is a token, and the model reads those bytes to fill in a [BLANK]. Change the text — in any language — and watch the byte count and the fill both react. Nothing leaves your device.
