Wild: find weird things by description

Because the query is just text, it doesn't have to name an object at all. Ask OWLv2 for something dangerous, the odd one out, or something you could sit on, and it boxes whatever its shared image-text space thinks matches. It won't always be right — that's the fun. Everything runs on your device.
