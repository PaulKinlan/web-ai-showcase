Do two passages mean the same thing?

The fundamental capability, felt directly: Jina turns each passage into a 768-d unit vector, and cosine similarity — the dot product of those vectors — is one number from −1 to 1 for how close in meaning they are. Because Jina is long-context, the passages can be whole paragraphs, not just sentences. Edit either side and watch the score move.
