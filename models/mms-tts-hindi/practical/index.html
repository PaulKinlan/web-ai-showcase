Practical: a Hindi narrator

The shape of a real read-aloud feature — Hindi content in, spoken Hindi out, entirely on-device. Pick a register below (a push notification, a weather report, an article snippet), tweak the text, and MMS narrates it. Nothing is uploaded and there's no per-character bill.

Download .wav
register – backend – gen time – audio –

One ~38 MB Hindi checkpoint covers every register — the model doesn't know or care whether it's reading a weather report or a warning; it just turns Devanagari characters into a waveform. That makes it a practical building block for an offline Hindi voice in any app.
