Wild: arithmetic on meaning

Because meanings live as vectors, you can do algebra on them. The classic: A is to B as C is to ? Compute B − A + C and find the phrase whose embedding lands closest. Nomic embeds every phrase with the classification: prefix and does the vector maths right here — no word2vec, no server.
