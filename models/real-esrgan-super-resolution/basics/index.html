Basics · image-to-image (super-resolution) · WASM

Shrink an image, then upscale it 4×

The plain capability: take a photo, shrink it to a small thumbnail, and let Real-ESRGAN rebuild a 4× larger version. Drag the divider to wipe between a plain bicubic enlargement and the Real-ESRGAN result — both at the same size.

Run it

◀ bicubicReal-ESRGAN ▶
backend –size –sharpness –

What you're seeing

Real-ESRGAN doesn't just interpolate between pixels — it learned what real high-resolution scenes look like and reconstructs edges and texture the thumbnail lost. Watch the sharpness number climb, and notice detail (window frames, railings, fabric weave) reappear that a bicubic "enlarge" could never recover.
