Rescue an over-compressed image

A small, blocky, over-compressed image is a common real-world problem — a legacy thumbnail, a screenshot saved too small, an asset from an old CMS. Real-ESRGAN was trained on exactly this kind of degradation: enlarge it 4×, clean up the JPEG blocking, and download a usable version. Entirely on your device — nothing uploaded.

What you're seeing

The bicubic side stays soft and keeps the compression blocks; the Real-ESRGAN side reconstructs clean edges and removes the blocking, because the network was trained to invert real JPEG and downscaling degradation. Download saves the full 4× result — no watermark, no upload, no cost.
