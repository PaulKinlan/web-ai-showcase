Find where the GAN starts inventing

Super-resolution reconstructs plausible detail — but the smaller the source, the more of that detail is invented rather than recovered. Shrink the source tiny and watch Real-ESRGAN hallucinate: texture that looks convincing but was never in the original. A vivid lesson in what a GAN upscaler can and can't do.

What you're seeing

At a moderate source size Real-ESRGAN recovers real structure. Crank the source down to 32–48 px and the model has almost nothing to work with — so it confidently manufactures fur, brick, or fabric texture that only looks right. That's the honest limit of super-resolution: it's an enhancement, not a source of ground truth. Never treat an upscaled image as evidence of what was actually there.
