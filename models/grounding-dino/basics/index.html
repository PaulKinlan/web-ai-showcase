Basics: ground your own phrases

Phrase grounding at its simplest. Write a caption made of phrases — separate them with periods — hit Detect, and Grounding DINO boxes every region each phrase matches, with a score. Change the words and the results change; no retraining, all on your device. Slide the threshold to trade recall for precision.

Grounding DINO runs once at a low floor; the slider just filters the boxes it already found. Changing the caption re-runs the model, because the phrases are re-encoded as new text.
