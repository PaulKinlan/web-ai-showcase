Wild: ground weird referring phrases

Because the prompt is just language, it doesn't have to name an object at all. Ask Grounding DINO for something dangerous, the odd one out, or something you could sit on, and it grounds whatever its fused image-text features think matches. It won't always be right — that's the fun. Everything runs on your device.
