Retrieval-augmented BLOOMZ

BLOOMZ is small, so it doesn't know your facts — but it can read. Here all-MiniLM-L6-v2 embeds your question and every note, picks the most similar one, and BLOOMZ answers grounded only in that retrieved note. That's Retrieval-Augmented Generation — the standard way to give a tiny model real knowledge — running entirely on-device.
