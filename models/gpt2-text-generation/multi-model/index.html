Multi-model: write, then read the mood

A generate-then-judge loop with two models on your device. GPT-2 continues your prompt; then a DistilBERT sentiment model reads the result and scores its mood. Generate a few times and watch how sampling swings the tone — some completions come out cheerful, others bleak.
