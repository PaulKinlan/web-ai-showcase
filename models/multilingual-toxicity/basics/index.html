Basics: score one line, in any language

The simplest thing this classifier does: text in, a single toxic-vs-neutral probability out. Type something (or pick a sample in Arabic, Spanish, Russian, German…), and the two-class softmax appears below — computed entirely on your device.

–
backend – latency – classes 2 (softmax)

The two probabilities are a softmax — they sum to 100%. A verdict of ALLOW at, say, 12% toxic still means the model saw some signal; the threshold is where you decide how cautious to be.
