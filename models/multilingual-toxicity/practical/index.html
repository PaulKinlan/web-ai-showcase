Practical: a cross-language moderation queue

The shape of a real moderation feature. Paste a stream of comments — one per line, in any mix of the 15 languages — set a threshold, and the classifier scores every line and sorts them worst-first, so a human reviews the most likely-toxic first and the benign majority is auto-approved. Nothing is uploaded.

total – flagged – allowed – time –

Sorted worst-first, a reviewer can stop reading the moment the scores drop below their comfort line. The threshold is a policy dial, not a truth: raise it to reduce false positives, lower it to catch more.
