Person re-identification — is it the same person?

Face recognition looks at a face. Person re-identification looks at the whole body — clothing, build, gait, colour — to decide whether two crops from different moments or cameras show the same individual. It's how retail analytics, sports tracking, and surveillance re-find a person after they leave one camera's view. YouTu-ReID maps each person crop to a 768-d embedding; the cosine similarity between two embeddings is the match score. Why raw ONNX Runtime Web?

Transformers.js has no person-reid model class or task, so we run YouTu-ReID's ONNX graph directly with onnxruntime-web and hand-write the pre/post a pipeline would own: crop each person to the fixed 128×256 input, pack it as [1,3,256,128] float32 in 0–1, and L2-normalise the 768-d output so cosine similarity is the match score. Everything runs on your device.
