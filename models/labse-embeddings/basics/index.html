Is this a translation? One cross-lingual number

The fundamental capability: type a source sentence and a few candidate lines in any languages. LaBSE embeds all of them into one 768-d space and scores each candidate against the source by cosine similarity. A real translation scores near 1.0; something unrelated stays low — even though the two sentences may not share a single character.

Try it

Green badge = a hit in a different language from the source. The distractor should sink to the bottom with a much lower score.

backend –latency –dims 768
