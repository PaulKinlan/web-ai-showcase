Cluster a mixed-language corpus by meaning

Here is something you could never do with a monolingual model: mix sentences from many languages, then group them by topic — and watch the clusters ignore language completely. LaBSE embeds every line into one 768-d space; k-means (cosine) on those vectors finds the topics. A Japanese weather sentence lands with a French weather sentence, not with the other Japanese lines.
