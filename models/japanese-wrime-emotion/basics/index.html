Basics: score one Japanese sentence across eight emotions

Emotion classification in its simplest form. Pick a Japanese sentence — or write your own — and the model ranks all eight emotions with a probability and the raw logit behind each, plus the SentencePiece pieces it split your text into.

backend –latency –entropy –

SentencePiece pieces — dashed = a token boundary (▁) the tokenizer chose in a spaceless sentence

Every score is a real, on-device forward pass. The eight emotions are Plutchik's set, the labels the WRIME corpus uses.
