Practical: an emotion board for a batch of Japanese text

A real triage tool. Paste a batch of Japanese lines — reviews, support messages, survey answers — and the model scores each one's dominant emotion, then groups and colour-codes them so you can route an angry message differently from an anxious or a disappointed one. Everything runs on your device.
