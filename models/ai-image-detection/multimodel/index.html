Multi-model: detect provenance, then describe

A moderation intake card needs two things: what is in the image and where it might have come from. Here two models run in sequence — the AI-image detector attaches a provenance flag, and ViT-GPT2 captioning writes a one-line description, streaming token by token. Together they turn a bare upload into a reviewable card. Both models run locally; nothing is uploaded.
