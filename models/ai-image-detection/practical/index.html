Provenance triage: sort a batch, not judge it

The shippable pattern. You have a pile of images and limited review time. Score them all locally, then rank by AI-generated probability so a human starts with the most suspicious. Nothing is blocked or accused automatically — the model just decides reading order. That's the honest way to use an imperfect detector at scale.
