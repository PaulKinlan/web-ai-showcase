Wild: thinking on vs off, side by side

Qwen3 is one model with two brains. Here you run the same prompt through both — a direct /no_think answer on the left, a reasoned /think answer (with its trace) on the right — so you can see, on your own hard question, exactly what reasoning buys: often a better answer, always more tokens and more time. This is the kind of A/B you could never do client-side before a hybrid reasoner ran in the browser.

/no_think Direct answer

The fast, no-reasoning answer appears here.
decode –tokens –time –

/think Reasoned answer

Thinking…
The reasoned answer (with a trace above) appears here.
decode –reasoning –answer –time –
