Basics: a streaming chat with a thinking toggle

The fundamental move: you type, Qwen3 answers, streaming on your GPU. Leave thinking on and it reasons in a trace first; turn it off for a fast, direct reply. Pick a prompt or write your own. WebLLM keeps everything on-device. (First load is a ~1.1 GB download; then it's cached.)
