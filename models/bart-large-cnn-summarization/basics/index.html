Basics: summarize, and dial the length

The simplest thing BART-large-CNN does: read an article and write a shorter version in its own words. Pick a sample, then drag the length budget up and down and re-summarize — short budgets give a headline, longer ones keep more detail. The summary streams in as it generates.

Because BART-large runs the full 12+12 layers, each summary takes a little longer than the distilled version — but the phrasing is usually cleaner and it drops fewer facts. Short budgets force harder choices about what matters.
