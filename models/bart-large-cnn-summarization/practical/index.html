Practical: TL;DR your own document

Drop in your own news article, report, or long email thread and get the gist back — as a flowing paragraph or as scannable bullet points. This is the everyday job: turn something too long to read right now into something you can act on in seconds, without sending a word to a server.

TL;DR

Bullet mode splits the generated summary into sentences after the fact — the model still writes a paragraph, this just reshapes it. For a very long document, summarize in sections and combine, or use the recursive demo to collapse it further.
