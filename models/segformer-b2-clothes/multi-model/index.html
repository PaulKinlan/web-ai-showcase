Multi-model: outfit → CLIP style

Compose two on-device models. SegFormer clothes parsing extracts just the garments — the outfit on a clean background, with the face, hair, and scene removed. Then CLIP scores that outfit against any style labels you type. The parse focuses CLIP on the clothing, not the person or setting. Two downloads, then everything runs locally.

Model 1 — SegFormer clothes parsing (~29 MB)

Model 2 — CLIP ViT-B/16 (~90 MB)

Drop a photo of a person, or click to choose

Extracted outfit (CLIP input)

CLIP style scores

Run to classify the outfit's style.
parse –CLIP –garment px –

Clothes parsing runs once to build the outfit cut-out; re-typing labels re-runs only CLIP over the cached cut-out. Nothing is uploaded.
