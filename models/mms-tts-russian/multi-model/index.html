Multi-model: translate, then speak Russian

Two models, one pipeline, zero servers. M2M100 (multilingual, English→Russian) translates what you type into native Cyrillic, then MMS-TTS Russian reads the translation aloud. Type English, get spoken Russian — entirely on your device.
