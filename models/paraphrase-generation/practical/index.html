Copywriting A/B — variants to test

Turn one line — a headline, a CTA, a subject line — into several alternatives you can A/B test. "Boldness" is temperature: safe stays close, bold ventures further. Each variant shows how much the wording changed; pick and copy the one you like. Nothing leaves your device, so it's safe to iterate on unreleased copy.
