Wild: air-draw

Your hand is the brush. Pinch your index finger and thumb together and move — the model tracks the pinch point and paints a line that follows it. Open the pinch to lift the pen. No stylus, no touchscreen, just your webcam and 21 landmarks.

delegate –pinch –pen up

Tip: keep your hand ~40–60 cm from the camera. The pen goes down when the pinch distance drops below the threshold, and up when you separate your fingers.
