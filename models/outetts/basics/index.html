Hear a language model speak

The fundamental capability, plainly: type a sentence, press generate, and OuteTTS says it back. Under the hood the Qwen2 LLM turns your words into a stream of audio tokens and the WavTokenizer codec turns those tokens into the waveform you hear. Watch the token count and generation time — they are the real cost of speaking as next-token prediction.
