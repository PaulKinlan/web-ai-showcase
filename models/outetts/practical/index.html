Narrate a passage, sentence by sentence

Real read-aloud needs more than one sentence. An LLM-based TTS has a token budget, so the robust approach is to split a passage into sentences, generate each, and stitch the clips together — you get progress as it goes and one clip at the end. This is exactly how you would build a private article-to-audio or accessibility read-aloud feature with nothing leaving the device.
