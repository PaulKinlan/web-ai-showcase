Summarise a passage, then speak it

A model that both reads and talks, entirely on-device. Stage 1: FLAN-T5 — an instruction-tuned seq2seq model — condenses your passage into a short summary. Stage 2: OuteTTS speaks that summary aloud. It is the skeleton of a "listen to the gist" feature: long text in, a spoken TL;DR out, nothing uploaded.
