Voice & style control — the LLM's sampling knobs

Here is what makes an LLM-based TTS unusual: the same knobs that make a chat model creative or steady also shape a voice. Low temperature gives a flat, consistent read; higher temperature loosens the delivery. And each speaker profile conditions the model like a persona. Generate the same sentence across a temperature sweep or across speakers and listen to the difference — something you could never do with a fixed vocoder.
