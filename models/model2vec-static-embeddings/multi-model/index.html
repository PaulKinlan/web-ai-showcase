Multi-model: Model2Vec prefilter → cross-encoder rerank

The standard production shape for good, fast search. Stage 1 — Model2Vec embeds the entire corpus in microseconds and takes the top few by cosine (a cheap, wide net). Stage 2 — a small but accurate cross-encoder (Jina reranker) reads each shortlisted item together with the query and reorders them. You get the speed of static embeddings and the precision of a cross-encoder, spending the slow model on only a handful of candidates. Both run locally.
