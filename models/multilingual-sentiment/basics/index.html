What to notice

  • The verdict stays the same across languages — that's the point of a shared multilingual encoder.
  • Confidence wobbles a little: the model saw more English and Spanish tweets than Hindi, so some languages are read more sharply.
  • Switch to the neutral message and watch every row slide toward the middle class.
