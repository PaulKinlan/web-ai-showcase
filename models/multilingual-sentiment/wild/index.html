Mixed languages, emoji-only, sarcasm

Clean single-language sentences are easy. Real social text is messy: half English half Spanish, a row of emoji with no words, heavy negation, deadpan sarcasm. This model was trained on tweets, so some of that is home turf — and some of it will fool it. Try the presets, then write your own trap.

What you're seeing

  • Emoji carry weight — because it's tweet-trained, 😍/🤬 alone often produce a confident verdict.
  • Code-switching mostly works — one shared encoder means a bilingual sentence still lands.
  • Sarcasm is the classic failure — "just great, another delay 🙃" reads literally positive to most models. Watch the word-pull strip to see why.
  • Negation is subtle — "not bad at all" flips the polarity of "bad"; sometimes the model catches it, sometimes not.
