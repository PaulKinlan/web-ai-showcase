Practical: a moderation queue with a threshold

The shape of a real content-moderation pipeline. Paste a batch of comments (one per line), pick a threshold, and the queue auto-sorts: anything scoring at or above it on any label is flagged for human review, the rest are auto-allowed. Flagged items float to the top, worst first.
