Basics: score a single comment

The capability in its simplest form. Give toxic-bert a comment; it hands back six independent probabilities — one per kind of toxicity. Bigger bar = more sure. Nothing leaves your device.

backend –latency –

These six labels are independent sigmoids, so they don't add up to 100% — a comment can score high on several at once.
