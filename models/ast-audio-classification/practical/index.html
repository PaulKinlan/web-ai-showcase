Practical: a sound-event monitor

Choose a sound to watch for. AST listens to your mic in ~2-second windows and flashes a big, obvious alert the moment it hears a match — a private accessibility alerter for anyone who can't rely on hearing an alarm, doorbell, or a crying baby. Audio never leaves the device.

Why this is only possible on-device

A cloud alerter would mean streaming your home's microphone to a server — a privacy non-starter and useless offline. Running AST locally means the audio never leaves the room, there's no latency round-trip, and it keeps working with no network.
