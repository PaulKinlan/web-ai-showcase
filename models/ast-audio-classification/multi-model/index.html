AST × Whisper: classify, then transcribe

A real pipeline of two local models. AST first decides what a clip is. If it's speech, it hands the audio to Whisper, which writes down the words. Anything else — music, a tone, a whistle — just gets tagged. This is how you'd triage a stream of audio without wasting an ASR model on non-speech, and without a server.
