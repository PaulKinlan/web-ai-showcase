Same scene, or not?

A shippable use case: decide whether two photos show the same scene — robust to crop, rotation, scale, and lighting. Confident correspondences that also agree on one geometry (RANSAC inliers) are the signal. Great for de-duplicating a photo roll or verifying a re-shot document.
