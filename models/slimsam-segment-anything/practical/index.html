Practical: click-to-cutout

A thing you could ship: a "magic select" cut-out tool. Click the subject and SlimSAM lifts it cleanly onto a transparent background — ready for stickers, collages, or product shots. The image never leaves the browser, and the exported PNG keeps real alpha.
