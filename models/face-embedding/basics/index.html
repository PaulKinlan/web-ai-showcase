Synthetic StyleGAN2 faces — no real people. Everything runs on-device; nothing is uploaded. Similarity is a fuzzy signal, never identity.

What just happened

FaceNet maps each 160×160 face crop to a point in a 128-dimensional "face space". The training objective pulled the same identity's photos together and pushed different identities apart, so the angle between two embeddings (their cosine similarity) is a good "same person?" signal. Nothing here is a database lookup — just two vectors and the angle between them.
