Multi-model: retrieve with I-JEPA, then classify with ViT

Self-supervised embeddings are great at "which of these look alike?" but they don't hand you a word for what a thing is. So chain two models: I-JEPA ranks a gallery by similarity to your query (no labels, no training), then a supervised ViT classifier names the retrieved image from ImageNet's 1,000 classes. Label-free retrieval, labelled answer — both on-device.
