Wild: sarcasm and the context it can't see

This model scores the surface of the text. It has no model of who is speaking, whether a line is quoted, or whether "this is sick" means good or bad. Sarcasm, quoted abuse, reclaimed insults, and neutral mentions of violent topics all pull the severity ladder the wrong way. Click a case and watch the distribution — the point is to see why you never wire this straight to an automatic ban.
