Basics: rate a single comment

The capability in its simplest form. Give the model a comment; it returns one softmax distribution over four severity classes. The bars compete — they add up to 100%. Nothing leaves your device.

These four classes are a single softmax, so they sum to 100% — the model commits to one severity for the whole comment.
