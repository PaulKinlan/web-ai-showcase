Practical: a comment queue sorted by severity

The shape of a real moderation pipeline. Paste a batch of comments (one per line), pick a threshold, and the queue auto-sorts by offensive-or-worse probability: anything at or above it is flagged for human review, the rest are auto-allowed. Worst first, so a reviewer starts where it matters. The model is one signal — a queue, not a judge.
