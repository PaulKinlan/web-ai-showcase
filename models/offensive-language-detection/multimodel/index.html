Multi-model: offensive vs hate vs toxicity

"Offensive", "hateful", and "toxic" are three different questions, and a real trust-and-safety stack often asks all three. Here three heads score the same comment at once: hate_speech_en (how offensive, on a severity ladder), twitter-roberta-hate (targeted at a protected group?), and toxic-bert (toxic / insult / threat?). Watch them agree on the clear cases and split on the interesting ones — a plain insult is offensive and toxic but not hate; polite bigotry is hate but barely "offensive". Three models, all local.

Heads up: this page loads three separate models (~109 + ~358 + ~110 MB). Each caches independently and auto-initialises on your next visit; the hate head is large, so first load is best on a good connection.
