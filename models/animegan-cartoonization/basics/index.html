Turn a photo into anime

The plain capability: pick a photo and an anime style, and let AnimeGANv2 repaint it. Drag the divider to wipe between the photo and its anime version.

Run it

◀ photoanime ▶
backend –style –latency –restyle amount –

What you're seeing

AnimeGANv2 doesn't apply a colour preset — it re-draws the scene: flat regions get smoothed, edges thicken into outlines, and skies and foliage are repainted with the trained animator's texture. Watch the "restyle amount" (mean per-pixel change) — it's far larger than any colour filter, because the model rebuilt the picture in a new style.
