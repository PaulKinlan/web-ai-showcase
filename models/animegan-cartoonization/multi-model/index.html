Anime sticker: stylize, then cut out

Repaint a photo as anime with AnimeGANv2, then use MODNet to cut the subject out onto a clean backdrop — an anime sticker with a transparent (or solid-colour) background. Two models, chained, entirely local.

Run it

Two models load below (AnimeGANv2 ~8 MB, MODNet ~26 MB). Pick a photo with a clear subject (a person or pet works best), choose a style and a backdrop, then run.

1 · AnimeGANv2 (cartoonization)
2 · MODNet (subject matting)

Anime sticker (subject cut out)

anime –matte –style –

Both models run on-device; the photo, the anime repaint and the cutout never leave the tab.

Why chain them

Stylization and matting compose naturally: repaint first, then isolate the subject, and you get a reusable anime sticker/avatar with a clean edge — drop it on any background. It's the on-device version of a "make me an anime avatar" tool, with no upload and each stage a real model in your browser.
