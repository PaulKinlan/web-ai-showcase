Make an anime avatar, then download it

A real workflow: drop your own photo (a selfie, a pet, a favourite view), pick a style, repaint it as anime, and download the PNG for a profile picture or avatar. Nothing is uploaded.

Why on-device

Anime-fying a selfie usually means uploading it to some app's servers. Here it's a static web page: private, free, offline once the ~8 MB style is cached. Drop your photo and the anime PNG downloads straight from the canvas — your face never left the device.
