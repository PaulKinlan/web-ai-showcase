Wild: cluster sounds by ear

No labels, no training — just structure. Embed every clip into WavLM's 768-dim feature space and project the vectors down to 2D with PCA. Clips that sound alike land near each other: the two English speakers drift toward one region, each other language toward its own. This is what "self-supervised features" buys you — geometry you can cluster, straight out of the box.
