Multi-model: SigLIP vs CLIP, head to head

Both models map images and text into a shared space; they differ in the read-out. This page loads both in your browser and scores the same image and labels through each — SigLIP's independent sigmoids on the left, CLIP's competing softmax on the right. The clearest place to see it: give them a set of labels where none truly fit.
