Practical: multi-label tagging & moderation

The shippable version. Because each label is an independent yes/no, SigLIP is a natural multi-label tagger: pick a threshold and every label above it becomes a tag. The same mechanism moderates — an image can trip several policy flags at once, or none. No dataset, no fine-tuning, no server: the classifier is a list of words plus a threshold you own.

…or drop / choose your own

Each label is wrapped in This is a photo of {label}. SigLIP scores run low (a strong match is often 0.1–0.4), so the tag threshold defaults low — slide it to trade precision for recall.
