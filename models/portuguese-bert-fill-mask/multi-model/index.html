Multi-model: Portuguese-native vs multilingual

Two masked-LMs, one sentence. BERTimbau spent its whole 29k-token vocabulary on Brazilian Portuguese; multilingual mBERT split a 119k-token vocabulary across ~104 languages. Both use [MASK], so we can run the exact same Portuguese cloze through each and measure where the specialist and the generalist agree — and where they don't. Everything runs locally.

Load both models

BERTimbau (~528 MB, fp32) loads first; the multilingual mBERT (~180 MB) loads on demand for the comparison. Both are real off-thread forward passes.
