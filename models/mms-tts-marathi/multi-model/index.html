Multi-model: translate, then speak Marathi

Two models, one pipeline, zero servers. M2M100 (multilingual, English→Marathi) translates what you type into native Devanagari, then MMS-TTS Marathi reads the translation aloud. Type English, get spoken Marathi — entirely on your device.
