Basics: type a Marathi line, hear it

The simplest thing an end-to-end TTS model does: text in, waveform out. Type some Marathi (or pick an example), press बोला, and the audio plus its real 16 kHz waveform appear below — all synthesised on your device, in native Devanagari script.

VITS predicts the waveform directly — no mel-spectrogram plus vocoder step. The real-time factor is measured: below 1.0× means it synthesised faster than the clip's own length.
