Practical: a Tamil narrator

The shape of a real read-aloud feature — Tamil content in, spoken Tamil out, entirely on-device. Pick a register below (a push notification, a weather report, an article snippet), tweak the text, and MMS narrates it. Nothing is uploaded and there's no per-character bill.

One Tamil checkpoint covers every register — the model doesn't know or care whether it's reading a weather report or a warning; it just turns Tamil characters into a waveform. That makes it a practical building block for an offline Tamil voice in any app.
