Wild: label text in one language with labels in another

Here's something only a genuinely cross-lingual model can do: classify a Japanese sentence using English labels, or a Chinese review with Spanish categories. Because mDeBERTa learned the entailment relation aligned across languages, the label and the text don't have to share a language at all. Mix and match below.
