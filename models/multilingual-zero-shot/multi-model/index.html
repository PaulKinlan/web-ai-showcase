Multi-model: structure and route a message at once

Two multilingual models, one pass, all on-device. XLM-RoBERTa NER extracts the entities (who, which org, where, when); mDeBERTa zero-shot routes the whole message to one of your topics. Together they turn a free-text message in any language into a structured, routed record — no server.
