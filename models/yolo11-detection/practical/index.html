Practical: count things in a photo

Detection is most useful when it becomes a number. Point YOLO11 at a photo, let NMS dedupe the candidates, and it tallies everything it sees by class — how many people, cars, chairs, bottles — a private, offline inventory counter. Pick a class to isolate just those boxes.

Drop an image, or click to choose

backend –latency –total shown –

NMS matters for a count: too low an IoU merges neighbouring objects into one and undercounts; too high leaves duplicate boxes and overcounts. Tune it to your scene, then read the tally.
