Basics: 8400 candidates, then NMS

Detection in its rawest form. Give YOLO11 an image and its dense head returns 8400 candidate boxes — a flood of near-duplicates. A score threshold drops the unsure ones; Non-Max-Suppression merges the overlapping ones. Slide both and watch a clean set of boxes emerge in one pass.
