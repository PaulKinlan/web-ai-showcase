Why chain them

The two models cover each other's blind spots. CLIPSeg has open-vocabulary spatial grounding — it finds "the animal on the left" without ever being trained on that phrase — but its output is a mask, not a name. ViT has no localization at all, yet it puts a confident ImageNet label on a tight crop. Pipe one into the other and you get "find the thing I described, then tell me exactly what it is." Swap ViT for OCR to read a "licence plate", or for a captioner to describe the region — same pattern.
