Where this ships

  • Product cut-outs — "the shoe", "the mug" onto transparent for a catalogue, no studio mask.
  • Privacy redaction — blur "a face", "the licence plate", "the screen" client-side before an image is stored or shared.
  • Background swaps — isolate the subject, drop any backdrop behind the transparent PNG.

CLIPSeg gives a soft, sometimes ragged mask — great for redaction and quick cut-outs. For a crisp matte you'd refine the edge (feather / matting), but the localisation is all from your words.
