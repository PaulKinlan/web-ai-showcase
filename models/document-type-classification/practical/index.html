Practical: a mailroom auto-sorter

The everyday business job. A stack of incoming scans lands; each needs to go to the right team — invoice to finance, resume to HR, form to intake. DiT reads each page's layout and drops it into a queue, and a confidence gate escalates the pages it isn't sure about to a human review lane instead of mis-filing them. Nothing leaves the browser.

Drop document images to add to the batch, or click to choose

Batch (0 pages) — click a page to remove it:

sorted –auto-filed –needs review –avg latency –

This is the shape of a real ingest pipeline: a cheap on-device classifier is the dispatcher, and the confidence gate decides what a human needs to look at. Raise the threshold and more borderline pages route to review; lower it and the machine files more on its own. The per-page confidence is the normalised top-1 probability from the softmax over all 16 types.
