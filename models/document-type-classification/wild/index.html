Wild: make the model argue with itself

Real scans are messy — a form stapled to a cover letter, a memo photographed at an angle, half a page. Here you build a Frankenstein document: paste the top of one type onto the bottom of another, slide the seam, rotate it, and watch DiT's confidence slide between types in real time. When the page is genuinely half-and-half, the distribution flattens and the entropy climbs — the model's uncertainty made visible.
