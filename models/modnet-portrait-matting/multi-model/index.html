Multi-model: matte → classify the subject

MODNet answers "which pixels are the subject?" ViT answers "what is it?" Chain them: matte the subject onto a clean backdrop, then classify only the cut-out. With the background removed, the classifier can't be swayed by the couch or the sky — recognition on the subject alone. Two models, one image, entirely on your device.
