Basics: soft matte vs. hard cut

The simplest MODNet move, and the clearest way to see why matting matters. Pull the matte, then flip between the soft cut-out (the raw alpha, hair intact) and a hard cut (the same matte forced to fully-in or fully-out at 50%). The hard cut is what a plain background remover gives you — watch the fringe of hair vanish.
