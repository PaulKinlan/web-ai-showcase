Practical: inventory tally

Not "is there a thing?" but "how many of each thing?" Point RT-DETRv2 at a shelf, a desk, a room or a street and it returns a full inventory: every object class it sees, a count per class, sorted busiest-first — the shape of a stock-take, a room audit, or a scene summary. Export it to CSV in one click. Still image or live webcam, nothing uploaded.
