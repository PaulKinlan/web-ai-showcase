Wild: crowd counter

A crowd is just a number waiting to be read. RT-DETRv2 boxes every person it can pick out of a dense scene and tallies them — this is where the improved decoder earns its keep, when bodies overlap and the boxes get tight. Drop your own crowd photo, or run the webcam for a live people counter. All on-device; the crowd never leaves your machine.

Crowd counting by detection has a ceiling — very distant or heavily occluded people are missed, and this is an r18vd (small) model. Lower the sensitivity to catch more faint boxes (at the cost of false positives). It's an honest live count of what the detector can resolve, not a density-estimation model.
