Wild: what it misses (the recall gap)

This model is conservative. It fires confidently on explicit, surface-level protected-group attacks — and stays quiet on almost everything else. Coded "dog-whistle" hate, pseudo-polite bias, and discriminatory policy statements with no slur mostly come back not_hate. That is the real failure mode here: not false alarms, but false silence. Click each case and watch how much genuinely harmful text it lets through — which is exactly why a not_hate result is not a clean bill of health, and why you never wire this to an automatic action.
