Basics: hate vs not-hate, and the target

The capability in its simplest form. Give the model a comment; it returns one softmax over eight classes — seven protected-group hate types plus not-hate. When hate wins, you also see which group is targeted. Nothing leaves your device.

–
backend –latency –

The eight classes are a single softmax, so they sum to 100% — nasty-but-not-targeted text lands on not hate, which is exactly what makes this narrower than toxicity.
