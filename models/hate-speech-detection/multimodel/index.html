Multi-model: hate vs toxicity, side by side

"Hateful" and "toxic" are different questions. Here two heads score the same comment: twitter-roberta-hate (is this targeted at a protected group, and which one?) and toxic-bert (is this toxic / an insult / a threat?). Watch them agree on clear group-directed hate — and diverge when text is toxic-but-not-hate (a plain insult) or hateful-but-calm (polite bigotry). Two models, both running locally.
