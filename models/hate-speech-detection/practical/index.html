Practical: triage queue — a signal, not a verdict

The shape of a real hate-moderation pass. Paste comments (one per line), pick a threshold, and the queue auto-sorts by hate probability, showing the targeted group for each. This is deliberately framed as a reviewer's queue: the model surfaces likely hate and where it's aimed, and a human makes the call. Never wire this straight to an automatic ban.
