Untangle the densest text you can find

Push it. Paste legal boilerplate, an academic abstract, insurance small print — the kind of writing built to be impenetrable — and see how far a small (~80M) model can plain-language it. This is the honest stress test: sometimes the reading-ease score jumps a long way, sometimes the model is conservative or lightly paraphrases. You see the real output either way, with the before/after numbers to judge it for yourself.
