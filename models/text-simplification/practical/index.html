A plain-language rewriter for accessibility

The real use case: paste a whole passage and rewrite it into plain language, sentence by sentence. Each sentence goes through the model separately (so long documents don't overflow the context), and the panel reports the readability of the whole passage before and after. Copy the plain version out when you're happy with it. Everything runs locally — safe for content you can't send to a cloud service.
