Wild: the persona engine

One tiny model, many voices. The only thing that changes between these characters is the system prompt — Gemma re-uses the same weights to role-play a pirate, a Shakespearean bard, a terse robot, or a cheery chef, streaming in character on your device. Pick a persona (or write your own) and chat.

Same model, same weights — the persona lives entirely in the system prompt Gemma folds into the first turn. A 270M model won't hold a character as tightly as a big one, but the steering is real and it all runs on your device.
