Wild: give it a personality with seed turns

DialoGPT has no system prompt — but you can still steer it. Because it just continues the EOS-joined history, you can seed the conversation with example turns written in a voice, and the model tends to keep that voice going. Pick a persona (or write your own seed turns), then chat and watch it stay loosely in character. This is few-shot conditioning, laid bare.

DialoGPT is small and un-aligned, so it won't hold a character as tightly as a modern model — it drifts, and sometimes ignores the persona entirely. That fragility is itself instructive: personality here is pure pattern-continuation, with nothing enforcing it.
