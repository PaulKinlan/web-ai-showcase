Multi-model: reply, then read its emotion

Two models on your device, back to back. DialoGPT answers your message; then an emotion classifier reads the reply and scores its mood across seven emotions. It's a tiny version of a real pattern: generate, then run a second model to gate or route the output by tone — all locally, no round-trips.
