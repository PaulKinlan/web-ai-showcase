Practical: multilingual tagging & moderation

The shippable version. Because each label is an independent yes/no, SigLIP 2 is a natural multi-label tagger: pick a threshold and every label above it becomes a tag. The same mechanism moderates — an image can trip several policy flags at once, or none. And because SigLIP 2 is multilingual, your tag vocabulary and policy labels can be written in the market's own language. No dataset, no fine-tuning, no server.

…or drop / choose your own

SigLIP 2 base (q8) scores run low — a strong match is often under 1% — so the threshold defaults low. Trust the ranking and slide the threshold to trade precision for recall.
