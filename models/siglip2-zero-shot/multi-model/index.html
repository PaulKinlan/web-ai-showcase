Multi-model: SigLIP 2 vs English-only CLIP

Both models map images and text into a shared space. This page loads both in your browser and scores the same image and labels through each — SigLIP 2's independent sigmoids on the left, CLIP's competing softmax on the right. Two differences show at once: the sigmoid-vs-softmax read-out, and what happens when you feed them non-English labels — SigLIP 2 was trained multilingually; OpenAI's original CLIP was not.
