What you're seeing

LaMa doesn't blur or clone-stamp — its Fourier convolutions let it continue structure and periodic texture (foliage, brick, water) across the hole, so the fill looks like it belongs. Everything outside your mask is copied through pixel-exact; only the painted region is rewritten.
