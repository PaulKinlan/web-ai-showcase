Privacy redaction: erase and fill, then save

The everyday case: something in a photo shouldn't be shared — a face, a license plate, a bystander, a screen. Paint over it, let LaMa fill the region with background, and download the cleaned image. The original never leaves your device.

Redaction happens entirely on-device — the original, the mask, and the filled result never leave the tab. For hard privacy guarantees, verify the saved image before sharing.

Why fill instead of blur/box

A black box or blur announces "something was here" and can sometimes be reversed. Inpainting replaces the region with plausible background, so the result looks like the object was never there — a cleaner, less reversible redaction, produced without uploading the sensitive image anywhere.
