Basics: tag one headline

The plain capability. Type a headline and the model names its topic with a confidence, and shows the full distribution across all ten desks. One forward pass, entirely in the tab.

Every keystroke re-runs the model (debounced). Because the label set is fixed and baked into the head, a single pass yields all ten probabilities at once — no candidate labels to supply, no per-label pass.
