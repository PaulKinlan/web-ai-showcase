Practical: a newsroom auto-tagging board

Wire feeds are a firehose. Paste a batch of headlines — one per line — and the model sorts each onto its desk, so an editor sees the morning's stories already grouped by section. One batched pass through the model; no story text leaves the tab.

backend – headlines – desks – latency –

Each headline is placed on the desk it scores highest for, and desks are ordered by how many stories landed there. In a real CMS you'd pre-fill the section dropdown with this suggestion — the editor just confirms or overrides, and the model gave the ordering for free, client-side.
