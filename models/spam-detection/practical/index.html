Practical: triage a whole inbox

The everyday job of a spam filter: sort a pile of messages into the ones you want and the ones you don't. Paste one message per line, hit triage, and watch the board fill in — each message scored on-device and dropped into Inbox or Junk with its confidence.

Each row lands the moment the worker finishes scoring it, so a big paste fills the board progressively rather than freezing the page. Nothing is uploaded — the whole inbox is classified in a Web Worker on your device.
