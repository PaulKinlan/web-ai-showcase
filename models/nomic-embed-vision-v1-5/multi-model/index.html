Multi-model: search → caption

Compose two on-device models. Nomic retrieves the gallery image that best matches your phrase (cross-modal search); then ViT-GPT2 writes a fresh caption describing that image. Retrieval finds it, captioning explains it — a mini image-search-with-descriptions pipeline. Two downloads, then everything runs locally.

Model 1 — Nomic Embed Vision + Text (~236 MB)

Model 2 — ViT-GPT2 captioning (~250 MB)
