The semantic axis

Because words and images share one space, you can build an axis from two phrases and slide every image along it. Type a left pole and a right pole — indoors ↔ outdoors, calm ↔ busy, nature ↔ city — and each photo is placed by how much closer it is to one pole than the other. A classifier you draw with two phrases.
