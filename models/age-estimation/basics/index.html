Basics: estimate one face

Age estimation in its simplest form. Give the model a face and it returns a probability for each of 9 age buckets, plus a single expected-age number (a probability-weighted average). From your camera, a sample, or your own photo. The image never leaves your device — and remember it's an imperfect, biased guess at apparent age, not a fact.

🔒 Frames are read locally and never uploaded or stored.
