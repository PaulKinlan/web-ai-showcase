Age over frames — how much a single read wobbles

A single-frame age read looks authoritative but isn't. This plots the expected age live, re-reading a webcam frame a few times a second — so you can watch it swing several years as your angle, expression, and lighting shift. That wobble is the whole point: it's why you should smooth over time and treat any one number as soft. Nothing is uploaded or stored.

🔒 Frames are read locally and never uploaded or stored.
