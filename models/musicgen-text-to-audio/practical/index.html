Practical: royalty-free loops & UI sounds

Two things a developer actually ships: a background loop for a video, game, or presentation, and a short UI sound (a chime, a success sting). Pick a job, tune it, generate, and download the WAV — bespoke, on-device, with no stock-music licence to clear.

Compute-heavy. Runs on the CPU (WASM). UI sounds are short and quicker; loops take longer. The progress bar tracks real audio-token decoding.
