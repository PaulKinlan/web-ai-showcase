Wild: mix two moods into one track

Pick two vibes — say "rainy jazz café" and "8-bit arcade" — and slide between them. The page composes a single blended prompt weighted by the slider and MusicGen generates the mash-up on your device. There's no cloud "music blend" API; this only exists because the model runs here.

Compute-heavy. Runs on the CPU (WASM). Keep it short to iterate on blends. The progress bar tracks real audio-token decoding.

Prompt sent to the model:

Download .wav
