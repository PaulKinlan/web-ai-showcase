Multi-model: segment → crop → classify

Two models, one pipeline, all local. Click a point and SAM 2 segments the object; the page crops it to a tight cut-out and hands it to MobileViT, which names it. Segmentation feeds recognition — "what is the thing I just pointed at?" — with nothing leaving the browser.
