Wild: refine thin & occluded objects

One click is rarely enough for a wispy plant stem, a chair leg behind a table, or an animal partly hidden by something else. SAM 2 lets you stack prompts: add foreground + points on the parts it missed and background − points on the bits it wrongly grabbed, and watch the mask tighten with each point.
