Multi-model: cross-lingual RAG

Two models, one pipeline, both in your tab. Stage 1: multilingual E5 embeds a mixed English + Chinese corpus and your question, and retrieves the most relevant chunks — across languages. Stage 2: GLM-Edge answers your question grounded only in those chunks, in the language you asked. Ask in English about a fact written in Chinese and it still works.
