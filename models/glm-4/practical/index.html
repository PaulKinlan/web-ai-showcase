Practical: bilingual text → JSON

The workhorse of shipped LLM features, made bilingual: hand the model unstructured text — in English or Chinese — and a schema, and get back structured JSON you can validate and store. Low temperature, a strict system prompt, on-device parsing; nothing leaves the tab.
