Why pair them

FLAN-T5 is strong at using text you give it, but it has almost no world knowledge baked in at 80M params — ask it a fact and it guesses. Embeddings fix that: they fetch the relevant snippet so the generator only has to read and rephrase, not remember. That division of labour — retriever finds, generator answers — is the core of every RAG system, here running fully offline.
