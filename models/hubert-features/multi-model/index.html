Multi-model: retrieve by sound → read it out

HuBERT can search, but it can't read — its output is features, not words. So chain it: HuBERT embeds your query and every library clip and ranks them by cosine similarity, then wav2vec2 transcribes the single closest match. "Find the clip that sounds most like this — and tell me what it says." Two different speech models, both on-device. (wav2vec2 is English-only, so a non-English winner transcribes imperfectly — honestly.)
