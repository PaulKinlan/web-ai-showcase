Basics: a streaming reasoning chat

The fundamental move: you type, Baguettotron reasons out loud inside a <think> block and then answers, streaming token by token on your device. Transformers.js keeps the conversation on-device — nothing is uploaded. (First load transfers the weights, then it's cached.)
