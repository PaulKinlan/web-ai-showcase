Practical: structure a note, and de-identify it

Two things a clinic or research team would actually ship. Structure turns a free-text note into grouped lists — problems, medications, procedures, measurements — a first pass for a human to confirm. De-identify masks the spans that identify a patient (Age, Sex, Date, person references) so the note can be shared or logged. Both run entirely on your device — the point of doing this in the browser.
