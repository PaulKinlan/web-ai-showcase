What's happening

Audio is resampled to 16 kHz mono and fed to the XLSR-53 encoder. It emits, per frame, a probability over 59 Latin-French symbols; we take the argmax, merge consecutive duplicates, and drop the blanks. No decoder generates the text — it's read directly off the frames, so the model can't invent an unspoken word.
