Normals vs depth

They're different questions about the same pixel. Depth asks "how far away is it?"; a surface normal asks "which way does it face?". A flat wall at a slant has smoothly changing depth but a single constant normal; a bumpy surface at constant distance has constant depth but wildly varying normals.
