Wild: relay translation across languages

In relay translation you bridge languages through intermediate hops. Here you chain a sentence through a path of languages — English → Japanese → Russian → Arabic → back to English — and watch meaning travel across scripts and (sometimes) drift. It's the game of "telephone" for 50 languages, running entirely on-device.

Each hop is a real mBART translation. The more hops and the more distant the languages, the more a phrase can bend — idioms especially. If the round-trip comes back close to where it started, the chain held together.
