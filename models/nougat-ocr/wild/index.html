Wild: pull the LaTeX out of the math

This is the trick nothing else does. Point Nougat at a page full of typeset equations and it returns them as LaTeX source — fractions, sums, norms, subscripts and all — that you can paste straight into a paper. Plain OCR turns the same page into a rubble of symbols.
