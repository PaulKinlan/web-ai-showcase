Multi-model: transcribe → summarise

Two models, one pipeline, zero uploads. Nougat reads a research page into Markdown; then DistilBART summarises that text into a few sentences. It's the whole "what does this page even say?" loop, running on your device.

Stage 1 — transcription (Nougat small, ~250 MB)

Stage 2 — summarisation (DistilBART CNN 6-6, ~250 MB)

Why chain them? Nougat turns a page of pixels into text a language model can read; the summariser then compresses it. Neither step touches a server — the page you drop, and everything derived from it, stays on the device.
