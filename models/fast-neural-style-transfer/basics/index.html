Repaint a photo as art

The plain capability: pick a photo and an artistic style, and let a fast-neural-style network repaint it. Drag the divider to wipe between the photo and its painted version.

Run it

◀ photopainting ▶
backend –style –latency –restyle amount –

What you're seeing

Each style is a whole feed-forward network trained to reproduce one painting's brushwork — mosaic tiles, pointillist dots, swirling strokes. Watch the "restyle amount" (mean per-pixel change): it's far larger than a colour filter, because the model rebuilt the picture's texture, not just its palette.
