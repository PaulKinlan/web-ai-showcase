Multi-model: moderate, then speak

Two models, one pipeline, zero servers. The multilingual toxicity model acts as a gate: it scores the text first, and only text that comes back clean is passed to MMS-TTS Arabic to be spoken aloud. Toxic text is held instead — a voice assistant shouldn't read abuse back to a room. Type Arabic (the toxicity model covers 15 languages, so English works too) and watch it take one of the two paths.
