Wild: where the score wobbles

A NSFW classifier outputs a probability, not a verdict — and on input unlike its training data (abstract patterns, unusual colour fields, heavy blur) that probability can drift toward the middle. These procedurally generated tiles are all obviously benign, yet they let you watch the model's confidence move. The lesson for content-safety work: never treat a single score as ground truth, and always keep a human in the loop near the threshold.
