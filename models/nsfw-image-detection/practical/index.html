Practical: an upload-gate gallery

How a moderated gallery actually behaves. Add images (the samples, or drop your own), pick a threshold, and each is screened: anything the model rates NSFW at or above the threshold is withheld — kept blurred and held for review — while the rest are shown. This is the avatar/attachment gate every UGC product needs, running entirely client-side.
