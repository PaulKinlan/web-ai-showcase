Practical: intent routing & ticket triage

Point zero-shot at a whole inbox and it sorts each message into the categories you define — the exact teams or queues your business uses — with no labelled training data and nothing to retrain when the categories change. Paste some messages, set your routing labels, and triage them all at once.

backend –latency –messages –

Because the labels are just words, you can A/B different routing taxonomies live — split "billing" into "refund" and "overcharge", add a "cancellation" queue — and re-triage instantly. No dataset, no retrain.
