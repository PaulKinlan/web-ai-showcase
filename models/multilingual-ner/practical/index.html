Practical: redact PII & extract entities — in any language

Two things you'd actually ship. Redaction: mask the people, places and dates in a document before it's shared or logged — client-side, so the raw text never leaves the tab. Extraction: pull the who-where-when out into a structured, deduplicated list your downstream systems can act on. Both work whether the document arrived in French, Portuguese or Chinese.
