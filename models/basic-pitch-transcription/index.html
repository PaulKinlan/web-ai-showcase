Music transcription (audio → notes)

The built pitch detection demo tracks one note at a time. Real music is polyphonic — many notes at once. Basic Pitch, Spotify's tiny (~0.3 MB) audio-to-MIDI model, listens to a whole mix and lights up every key it hears as a piano roll. Play a sample, drop in an audio clip, or record from your mic — the model runs entirely on your device; nothing is uploaded.

Why raw ONNX Runtime Web?

Transformers.js has no music-transcription task, so we run Basic Pitch's ONNX graph directly with onnxruntime-web: resample audio to 22050 Hz mono, feed it in ~2 s windows, and read the model's note, onset, and contour heads. The piano roll above is the model's raw note output — every lit cell is a pitch the network heard at that moment. It's polyphonic by design, so chords and two-hand playing show many keys at once. Everything runs on your device.
