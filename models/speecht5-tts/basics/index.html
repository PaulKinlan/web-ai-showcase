Basics: text in, a chosen voice out

The simplest thing SpeechT5 does: read a line of text in a voice you pick. The voice is a 512-number speaker embedding — swap the speaker and the same words come out in a different voice.

Download .wav

Speaker x-vector used (the "who"):

backend –voice – gen time –audio –

SpeechT5 was trained on short utterances, so it's happiest with a sentence or two. Notice the x-vector changes completely when you change speaker — that vector is the entire identity of the voice.
