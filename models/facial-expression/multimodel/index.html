Detect the face, then read it

Two models in a row. First MediaPipe FaceDetector (BlazeFace, ~230 KB) finds the face and crops to it; then the emotion ViT reads the expression on that tight crop. Isolating the face first makes the read more robust than throwing a whole busy frame at the classifier — and it all runs on-device.
