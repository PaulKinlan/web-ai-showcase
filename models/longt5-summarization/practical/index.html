Practical: headlines & TL;DRs

The everyday shipping use: squeeze an article down to a single-line gist you'd put in a feed card, a push notification, or an email preview. Set a tight budget and LongT5 gives you the one-sentence version. Nothing is uploaded and there's no per-call bill.

One-line gist
in→out –compression – backend –time –

Tight budgets push the model toward its Pegasus-style, one-sentence gist behaviour. Because this is the base checkpoint, expect the gist to lift and lightly compress the most salient sentence rather than invent a punchy headline — still handy for previews, digests, and notifications.
