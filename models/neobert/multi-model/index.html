Three encoder generations, one query

NeoBERT (2025, a from-scratch next-gen base encoder), GTE-ModernBERT (2024, ModernBERT contrastively fine-tuned for retrieval) and MiniLM (2020, a distilled classic BERT). Give a query and a corpus; each model ranks the passages on your device. Read how the rankings line up — and where a fine-tuned embedder pulls the answer to the top more decisively than a raw backbone.
