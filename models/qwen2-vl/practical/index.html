Practical: read a document, answer a question

Qwen2-VL's strong suit is reading. Drop a receipt, invoice, form, or app screenshot and ask a question — "what's the total?", "what's the due date?", "which field is empty?" — and it transcribes and reasons over the text, on your device, with nothing uploaded.
