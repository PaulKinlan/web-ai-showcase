Multi-model: VAD → Whisper, transcribe speech only

This is why VAD earns its keep. Whisper is expensive; silence is free to skip. Silero finds the speech segments first, then Whisper transcribes only those — so the transcriber never burns time on dead air. Two models, both on-device: a tiny recurrent VAD and a Transformer ASR, composed.

Two models, both local

Silero VAD (~2 MB, onnxruntime-web) segments; Whisper-base (transformers.js) transcribes. Both auto-initialise from cache on a return visit.
