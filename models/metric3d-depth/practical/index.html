Practical: measure distances in metres

Metric3D predicts depth in real metres, so you can measure — not just guess "nearer / farther". Estimate depth once, then tap a point to read its distance in metres, or two points to get the gap between them. This is the core of a tape-measure-from-a-photo tool. Runs on WebGPU or WASM.

Drop a photo, or click to choose

Click / tap a point on the photo after estimating depth. Tap a second point to measure the distance gap.
