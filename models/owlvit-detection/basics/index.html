Basics: detect your own labels

Open-vocabulary detection at its simplest. Type the objects you want as free text, hit Detect, and OWLViT boxes every region that matches — with a score. Change the words and the results change; no retraining, all on your device. Slide the threshold to trade recall for precision.

OWLViT runs once at a low floor; the slider just filters the boxes it already found. Changing the query text re-runs the model, because each phrase becomes a new text embedding.
