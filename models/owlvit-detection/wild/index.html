Wild: abstract & rare queries

Object names are the easy case. Because OWLViT scores regions against any text embedding, you can ask for concepts — "something soft", "the odd one out", "something you could read". The matches (and the misses) are a peek into what CLIP space actually encodes. Lower the threshold to see the model's weaker guesses, raise it to keep only the confident ones.
