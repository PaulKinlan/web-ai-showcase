Rescue a small, compressed avatar

A tiny, over-compressed profile picture is a common problem — a legacy avatar, a video-call still, a face saved too small. GFPGAN reconstructs a clean face from it: restore, then download a usable version. Entirely on your device — the face is never uploaded.

What you're seeing

The input side is small and blocky; the restored side reconstructs a clean face because GFPGAN was trained to invert exactly this kind of real-world degradation. Download saves the full 512×512 restored face — no watermark, no upload, no cost. Remember it's a plausible reconstruction: great for a nicer avatar, not for identity verification.
