What you're seeing

GFPGAN doesn't just sharpen — it uses a StyleGAN2 face prior to reconstruct plausible eyes, skin, and hair the degradation lost. Watch the sharpness number climb and facial features become defined. Because it draws on a learned prior, the restored face is a plausible reconstruction, not a guaranteed likeness — a rescue, not forensic proof.
