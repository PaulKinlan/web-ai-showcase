Practical: feed hygiene triage

A reader app or newsroom dashboard gets a stream of headlines. Run each through the clickbait pre-check on-device and badge the manipulative ones so a human can read them critically — a media-literacy signal, never an automatic block. Slide the threshold to trade sensitivity for precision, and watch the queue re-triage instantly.

Read this as a signal, not a sentence

A punchy headline from a serious outlet can trip the flag; a scam can write a flat, "legitimate"-looking one. The right product move is to surface the cue — a badge, a "read critically" nudge, a sort option — and keep a human in the loop. Because it runs locally, none of the reader's feed leaves the device.
