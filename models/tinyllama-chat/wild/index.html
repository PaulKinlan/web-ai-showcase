Wild: constrained creative writing

Constrained writing — where the fun (and the difficulty) is a hard rule the text must obey: an acrostic that spells a word down the margin, a lipogram that bans a letter, a strict form. Small models are charmingly hit-and-miss at this, which makes it a great way to feel the sampler: crank the temperature for wilder attempts, drop it for careful ones. Runs on-device on your GPU or CPU.
