Wild: long-form transcription as seekable segments

Turbo's pruned decoder makes longer audio practical in the browser. Transcribe a clip and it comes back as timestamped segments — click any line to jump the player right to that moment. The playing segment highlights as the audio moves. It's the start of a searchable transcript viewer, running fully on-device.
