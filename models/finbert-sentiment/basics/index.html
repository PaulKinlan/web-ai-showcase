Basics: three-way financial tone, live

The simplest thing FinBERT does: read a financial sentence and decide whether it sounds positive, negative or neutral to a market participant, with a confidence for each. Type in the box and the verdict updates a moment after you pause. Flip "raised" to "cut" and watch the meter swing.

– confidence –
positive neutral negative
backend –latency –

Because it was tuned on formal financial statements, FinBERT is confident on headlines and earnings language and wobblier on chatty, sarcastic, or off-domain text. Check the confidence, not just the label — and remember it reports tone, not a trade.
