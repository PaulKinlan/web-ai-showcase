Wild: live crowd-density meter

Point your camera at a busy scene and watch D-FINE read the crowd. It counts a chosen class — people by default — every frame, and turns the count into a live density band: quiet, busy, or packed. A SOTA real-time detector accurate enough to trust the count, running in a tab; every frame is detected and drawn locally, nothing uploaded. No camera? Scan a sample instead.
