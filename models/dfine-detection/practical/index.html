Practical: inventory counter

Count stock the way a person walking a warehouse would — one photo at a time. Pick a class (bottle, car, book…), scan a photo, and D-FINE adds that count to a running inventory log with a grand total. Scan a shelf, then the next, then the next: the tally accumulates. This is the shape of a real stock-take, parking survey, or shelf audit — accurate, fast, and nothing ever leaves the device.
