Image credits & provenance

Every sample image on this site is accounted for by content hash in a machine-readable provenance ledger, and the licensed source images live in the rights-safe media library (see its README). This page renders that ledger.

Fail-closed people & rights policy
  • Every tracked image must have a provenance entry — an unrecorded image fails CI.
  • Any image showing an identifiable person must be clearly licensed (Public Domain / CC0 / CC-BY / CC-BY-SA) with a verifiable source, creator, and license — no face of unverified origin ever ships.
  • Faces and people are sourced from Wikimedia Commons or documented media-library assets; illustrations, mock documents, and synthetic textures are generated first-party for this project.
